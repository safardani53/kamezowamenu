The Actress Stuns In Red Carpet Snaps &amp; More Hollywood Life

Emma Roberts pictured at the HBO premiere Very Ralph at the Paley Center for Media on November 11, 2019, in Beverly Hills, CA.  

Emma Roberts, born on February 10, 1991, is an American actress and singer from Rhinebeck, New York. She made her film debut in the film Blow (2001), and went on to gain notoriety as the lead role on the Nickelodeon television comedy series Unfabulous (2004–2007), in which she released her debut studio album, Unfabulous and More, in 2005. She went on to star in a number of popular films including Aquamarine (2006), Nancy Drew (2007), Wild Child (2008), Hotel for Dogs (2009), Valentine’s Day (2010), It’s Kind of a Funny Story (2010), and The Art of Getting By (2011). Emma’s more serious films include roles in Lymelife (2008), 4.3.2.1. (2010), Scream 4 (2011), Adult World (2013), We’re the Millers (2013), Palo Alto (2013), The Blackcoat’s Daughter (2015), Nerve (2016), Who We Are Now (2017), and Paradise Hills (2019). Despite her impressive film resume, Emma gained even more fame for her roles in multiple seasons of the FX’s horror series American Horror Story (2013–present) and for her lead role on the Fox comedy horror series Scream Queens (2015–2016). In 2019, Emma began dating fellow actor Garrett Hedlund. In August 2020, Emma confirmed that the couple were expecting their first child, a baby boy.
