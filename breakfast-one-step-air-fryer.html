I make the perfect breakfast in my Air Fryer in one step - Ill never go back to using a frying pa

UNLESS you've been living under a rock - you probably already know loads of people who rave about their air fryer.

If you don't own one already, this food fan's quick and easy breakfast might convince you to make the purchase.

Paul, who is a professional hairdresser, shared a clip of him making a delicious meal on his TikTok account.

He revealed: "I'm not going to lie, I am fairly new to the whole air fryer thing.

"But the fact that it cooks a bacon and egg on a piece of toast and makes the bread taste kind of like fried bread is just too much."

He continued: "I'm going to show you what I do and if this isn't enough to convince you to get one, I don't know what is."

The food fan showed himself laying a slice of bread in his air fryer and placing bacon around the edge, leaving space in the middle for an egg.

Paul then squished the middle part of the bread down slightly with his finger and cracked an egg into it.

He said: "Just put it all in like that and put some salt and pepper on"

After 12 minutes at 160 degrees the snack was ready and looked mouth wateringly good.
