Who stars in the Frito-Lays Super Bowl commercial?

COMPANIES everywhere are pulling out the stops for Super Bowl 2022, and Frito-Lays is joining in on the fun.

Ahead of the big game on February 13, the popular snack business released a preview of their star-studded commercial, which will air during the NFL festivities.

Who stars in the Frito-Lays Super Bowl commercial?

On Friday, February 4, Frito-Lays gave viewers another sneak peek on YouTube of what to expect from their Super Bowl 2022 advertisement.

The short clip starts with an explorer who drops their bags of chips in the middle of the jungle.

All of the animals proceed to take some chips for themselves.

As Push It by Salt-N-Pepa plays, more animals grab a share of the explorer's dropped chips.

Two of the animals crowding the abandoned snacks are voiced by Megan Thee Stallion and Charlie Puth.

Megan also appeared in another short snippet distributed by Frito-Lays on January 24.

During the preview, the music star is enjoying a bag of Doritos until an unseen animal swipes her trailer on a production set.

Frito-Lays executive, Rachel Ferdinando, said of the casting: "Music has a way of bringing joy, connecting us and delivering a story in such a unique way."

Ferdinando added: "So reimagining 'Push It' with the help of Megan and Charlie will really allow our fans to unleash their Flamin' Hot."
