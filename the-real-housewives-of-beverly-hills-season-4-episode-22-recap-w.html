The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills Season 4 Episode 22 Recap: Who Won the Reunion?

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills Season 4 Episode 22 marked the end of the show’s three-part reunion special, and boy, was this one for the ages.

While it may not compare to the How I Met Your Mother finale, watch The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills online and prepare to get a little bit emotional.

Thanks to Ken Todd.

Watch The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills Season 4 Episode 22 Online

Right out of the gate, he apologized to Kim for making comments about her sobriety and to Yolanda for calling her stupid before even being asked about it.

Ken’s apology seemed very genuine, as did the way in which spoke about his relationship with Brandi Glanville, even if he was a bit less apologetic there.

Even in her case, Ken went from saying flat out that he could never be friends with her again to hugging her and crying on camera in a matter of minutes.

Touching stuff, Bravo.

While Ken was the consensus winner of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills Season 4 Episode 22, there was plenty of other drama to go around.

 

Brandi told Andy Cohen that she can’t pinpoint the part in their relationship with Lisa Vanderpump where things went wrong, but it involves Scheana Marie.

That much is for sure.

One of Lisa’s waitresses at SUR (and star of Vanderpump Rules), Scheana slept with Brandi’s ex-husband Eddie Cibrian while they were still married.

Brandi accuses Lisa of manipulating them to get them in the same room to cause drama. Lisa says she could never try to manipulate such a strong woman.

Kyle also had major beef with Lisa, and boy did she come prepared, saying Lisa was planning to "take her down" when she brought up the rumors.

Lisa claims she said she didn’t believe the rumors about Mauricio, and only brought it up on camera to diminish the story. Lisa did apologize, though.

While Mauricio appreciates Ken standing up for him, he’s not so pleased with Lisa, who said she never knows "for sure" about what goes on in a marriage.

Mauricio also didn’t like how Lisa made a joke about him "being with a younger woman," referring to their five year-old daughter, Portia, so there’s that.

Brandi continues to say that she does not want to be friends with someone (Lisa) who is friends with the woman (Scheana) who broke up her marriage.

Lisa keeps repeating that Scheana Marie is her employee, not her friend, but Brandi points out how close the two seem on Vanderpump Rules.

Basically, she wanted Lisa to say she would choose Brandi Glanville over her ex’s mistress, and was hurt when she didn’t and simply made a lot of excuses.
