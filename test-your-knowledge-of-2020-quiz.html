Test your knowledge of 2020 with quiz guru and EastEnders star Shaun Williamson

THE countdown to the end of 2020 is on...and thankfully it’s just five days away.

But just how well do you remember this most challenging year of all?

Quiz guru Shaun Williamson – who played Barry in EastEnders until 2004 – tests you on just how good your 2020 vision is.

Shaun, 55, said: “Lockdowns have given us a shared love of quizzing.

"It was the perfect way for families to connect virtually and share our twin passions of drinking and being right.”

  • Who did Emma Corrin portray in the Netflix drama The Crown?
  • On April 21, the Queen celebrated which birthday?
