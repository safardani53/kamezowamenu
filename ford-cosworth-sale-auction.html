Iconic Ford Sierra RS Cosworth up for sale at auction - less than 24 hours to go to pick up a bargai

AN Iconic Ford Sierra RS Cosworth is up for sale at auction, and classic car fans have less than 24 hours to pick up this bargain "modern classic".

The iconic 1987 Ford Sierra RS Cosworth, finished in white, has only 55,986 miles on the clock.

The Sierra RS Cosworth was the first Ford to wear "Cosworth" badging and was launched as a homologation model for Ford's racing aspirations.

The 'Cossie' has a turbocharged 2.0-litre petrol engine, producing 201bhp, while it has a set of 16-inch gold-coloured split-rim BBS wheels with Bridgestone tyres.  

This example includes Recaro front seats, electrically operated windows and mirrors, air conditioning, and a CD player.

It's been fitted with several modifications, including an aftermarket boost gauge, air intake, blow-off valve, and silicone hoses.

Collecting Cars, who are managing the sale via their bespoke website, said: "The paintwork is said to be in good condition, and the car has previously been resprayed in areas.

"There are stone chips on the front bumper, cracks in the front spoiler, and a small blemish on the bonnet.

"Inside, save for signs of use on the seats, entry sills, and door cards, the nostalgic cabin presents in good order.  

"The wing mirror controls are non-functional, though the seller is not aware of any further mechanical or electrical faults. "
