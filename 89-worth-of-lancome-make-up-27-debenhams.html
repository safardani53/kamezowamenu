Nab 89 worth of Lancome make-up for less than 30 with this one neat trick

BEAUTY junkies can get £89 worth of Lancome make-up for less than £30 with this clever hack.

Debenhams are currently offering a beauty free gift worth £57.33 with the purchase of two Lancome products.

The bargain hunters over at Money Saving Expert have worked out that the cheapest item available at the moment is a brow highlighter, which is for sale at £13.60 - reduced from £16.

By purchasing two 'Monsieur Big' brow highlighters for £27.20, you can then get the beauty gift for free.

The gift is made up of five separate sample sized items, which the MSE team have worked out as being worth almost £60.

The L'Absolu Sheer 202 lipstick, 1.65ml is worth £10 based on full-sized 4.2ml version, and the 1.5ml Monsieur Big mascara is worth £3.94 based on full-sized 8ml version.

There is also a 5.5ml Cils Booster mascara which is a full-sized (just small) product, that is normally £23.50.

As well as make-up, there is a 15ml Hydra Zen day cream worth £8.89, and a 5ml
Hydra Zen eye cream worth £11.

Totting up the value of the eyebrow pencils at their full £16 price tag, and the value of the free gifts, it means that you are getting a whopping £89.33 of make-up for a third of the price.

However, those looking to make the most of the deal should act fast.

The deal went live on January 29 and will run as long as stocks last.
