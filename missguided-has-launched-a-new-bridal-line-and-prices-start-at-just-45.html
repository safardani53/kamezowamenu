Missguided has launched a new bridal line, so can YOU tell which dress is 45 and which is the 1,

MISSGUIDED has launched a new bridal line and it’s perfect for brides on a budget.

The costs of tying the knot can add up fast.

One of the dresses, £45, above, is even a dupe for a high end Pronovias gown - which retails at approximately £1,300 on some websites.

From venues to flowers to catering, with a big bill heading your way, it’s always nice to save money where possible.

Missguided have just launched a new collection of bridal dresses – and they’re pretty purse friendly.

Although relatively cheap, the dresses don’t scrimp on style and there are numerous elegant options up for grabs, with prices starting at just £45.

All of the online retailer’s bridal dresses come in at under £150 and there both are longer and shorter style available.

And they’ve also included dresses for bridesmaids - with a wide range of fabrics, fits and colours.Bridal White Strappy Sequin Embellished Fishtail Maxi Dress 

Again selling for cheap and cheerful prices, the bridesmaid’s dresses could set you back as little as £30.
