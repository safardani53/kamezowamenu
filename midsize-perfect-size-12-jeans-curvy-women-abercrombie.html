Im midsize &amp; found the perfect pairs of size 12 jeans for curvy women from Abercrombie

A MIDSIZE woman has found the perfect pairs of size 12 jeans from Abercrombie that are comfortable and cute.

She shared a video geared toward the curvy community containing an in-depth review of jeans and the problems they sometimes bring.

TikTok user Kate, also known as @getittogetherkate on the platform, lets her funny side come to the forefront in a try-on video where she tells viewers her favorite pairs of jeans that are curvy girl-approved.

According to the woman's bio on the platform, much of her social content pertains to a "real girl aesthetic," consisting of size 12 clothing hauls, body-positive visuals, a glimpse into her professional life, and more.

In the Tiktok video, the midsize woman tries on four pairs of Abercrombie & Fitch jeans that she loves for curvy women, testing out features like their length, waist width, and sit-down quality, among others.

She makes the tone of her message even more clear in the appropriate video caption that reads "people who don’t like jeans simply haven’t found comfortable ones."

In an effort to provide transparency regarding size and fit, Kate shares that she is a size 12 and 5'11" with a 44-inch hip and a 32-inch waist.

The first style she shows viewers is the Curve Love Low Rise 90s Baggy Jean, $100, in Black Destroy that she says are "super comfy, super stretchy" and are also "sitting jeans."

She then adds that she got them in a size 32 instead of her normal 31 in the fashion company because she wanted them to feel a little more loose on her body shape.

The next piece she introduces is the light denim High Rise 90s Relaxed Jean, $89, in a size 32, which she claims are the best jeans to ever exist and are her "number one" choice.

"They absolutely make the booty pop," she states as she turns her backside to the camera and slaps her bum.

The digital creator then moves onto the third pair of jeans, which are not from Abercrombie but actually jeans from the brand Wranger found at her local thrift store.

"These are Wranglers from the thrift store, the men's department. They kinda make the booty saggy, but I do not give a single f**k," she declares.

She takes this time to offer some shopping advice and encourages female viewers to "check out the men's department" if they have trouble fitting into jeans or finding their respective size at a thrift store.

Kate brings her attention back to Abercrombie by exhibiting the last pair of jeans from her try-on, the High Rise Loose Jean, $90, in a size 32 and a dark wash, which she says she loves for its comfortable sitting ability.

The tall size 12 woman's commentary was well received by viewers with many leaving positive anecdotes on the video.

"Yesss, tall curvy gals unite," one viewer commented.
