DWTS crowns Xochitl Gomez as season 32 champ- but fans rip rigged results &amp; rage Jason Mraz wa

DANCING with the Stars fans have grown furious on social media after the winner of season 32 was announced.

Xochitl Gomez was announced as the winner of this season's Mirror Ball.

This year, DWTS surprised fans by putting five couples through to the final round.

Pop singer Jason Mraz, 46, was matched with professional dancer Daniella Karagach.

Reality television star and Vanderpump Rules hero Ariana Madix, 38, danced with Pasha Pashkov.

The most recent Bachelorette Charity Lawson, 27, performed with her partner Artem Chigvintsev.

Marvel superhero Xochitl Gomez, 17, was partnered with Val Chmerkovskiy.

Finally, How I Met Your Mother and Buffy the Vampire Slayer actress Alyson Hannigan, 49, tangoed with Sasha Farber.

SLAMMED

Fans of DWTS on X, formerly known as Twitter, ripped the show after the winner was announced.

One said: "RIGGED SORRY ARIANA ROBBED."

"Ariana deserved second place, she was robbed," said another.

More fans said: "Jason and Daniella were robbed" and "Jason Mraz was robbed."

A fifth wrote: "Just my opinion but Jason was given some tough choreo the whole season, was given difficult ballroom dances that other stars weren’t given (argentine tango), showed that when you’re knocked down to get back up again, AND always gave 110%… so yeah HE WON."

Other fans came after Xochitl because they thought she made a mistake in one of her final performances.

One said: "Can we call out @carrieanninaba and the other judges for not calling out Xochitl on her mistake?

"I’ve never watched before this season and I’d hate to find out that #DWTS is rigged."

Another added: "Of course when Xochitl messes up the judges turn a blind eye. This finale is rigged."

CRYSTAL BALL

Heading into the final episode, television star Alyson was believed to have put a spell on viewers, but that might have all changed after an emotional personal note from another finalist.

Jason seems to have won hearts, and votes, after praising his dance partner online.

In a message posted to Instagram, he stood next to a poster of his partner and gushed over her.

"Hey Daniella," the I'm Yours singer started. "No matter what happens, you're a winner. I’m so happy that I could assist in getting you to the finale yet again."

After that, Jason started to get emotional, and he had to fight back tears.

"I'm a crier, alright, and you made me cry. For a lot of reasons. For good and bad. I love you."

Fans on a Reddit board loved his message.

"OMG I'M SOBBING!!!" one person exclaimed.

Another said: "OMG I’m crying. I love love the couples this year. It’s so amazing to see the connections formed!! Jason is such a sweetheart, I can’t."
