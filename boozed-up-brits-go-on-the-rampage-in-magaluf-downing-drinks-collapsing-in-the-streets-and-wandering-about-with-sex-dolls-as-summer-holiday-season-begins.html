BOOZY Brits shed their clothes and inhibitions when they descended on the bars and clubs of the Spanish party town of Magaluf this weekend.

These eye-popping pics show raucous revellers hit nightspots as the holiday season kicks off with a bang.

And one group of lads took things even further - getting  fined by cops for running along the sea-front of a popular holiday resort in nearby Calvia completely naked.

The party town saw thousands of tourists fly from the UK to spend a week downing drinks and letting their hair down.

Thousands of people were pictured this weekend stumbling out of bars and clubs along the main strip of the town in all manner of ridiculous costumes.

Scantily-clad women and shirtless men posed for the camera on popular Punta Ballena street.

In nearby Calvia, a large group of lads were spotted  running along the sea-front of a popular holiday resort completely naked.

Of the 20 who bared all in the daylight escapade, 18 have been fined for violating local byelaws on "co-existence". Another  was sanctioned for obstructing the traffic.

It is not known how much the Brits were fined but the penalty can be up to €600 each.

According to the local council, the men have all apologised for their behaviour.
