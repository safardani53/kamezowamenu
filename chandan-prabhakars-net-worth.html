What is Chandan Prabhakars net worth?

ONE of India's best and brightest comedians and actors Chandan Prabhakar has been making a name for himself in the TV and movie industry since he started his acting career over a decade ago.

Now Chandan, 41, has settled down with a family and is apart of a long running TV series alongside his childhood friend Kapil Sharma that's seen him make a very nice paycheck.

What is Chandan Prabhakar’s net worth?

How long was Chandan Prabhakar in the Kapil Sharma Show?

Chandan was in the popular Hindi TV series for six years between 2016 to 2022.

His character in all five seasons was called Chandu - something the actor now gets refereed to a lot by his fans.

So far there have been 472 episodes with the final one airing 23 July 2023.

He has appeared in 237 of the episodes making Chandan the sixth most viewed star only behind Kapil Sharma, Kiku Sharda, Sumona Chakravarti, Archana Puran Singh and Krishna Abhishek.

Comedian Kapil Sharma leads the show as he talks with celebrity guests about their latest films and keeps fans engaged with his wit, humour and clever skits.

What movies and TV shows has Chandan Prabhakar appeared in?

Outside of The Kapil Sharma Show, Chandan has also had starring TV roles in Comedy Circus Ke Ajoobe back in 2012 as well as appearing on another Kapil Sharma show - Comedy Nights.

But it's his movie career where he's found more success and been able to translate his hilarious comedy into people's homes.

His first ever proper role came as one of fictional character Thakur's sons in Bhavnao Ko Samjho in 2010.

The action comedy remains his lowest rated movie as his next three all performed well with viewers and critics alike.

Power Cut in 2012 was his first big mainstream success as the rights were bought by Amazon Prime and streamed on their platform.
