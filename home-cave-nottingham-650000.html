Inside incredible 650,000 home that comes with its own swimming pool, steam room, jacuzzi and CAVE

THIS luxury family home boasts an incredible feature Fred Flintstone would be proud of - its very own cave.

Made from reinforced fibreglass and built into the landscaped garden, it houses a bar, kitchen and spa as well as plenty of space to have a gay old time in.

The four-bed property in Edwinstowe, Notts, is on the market for £650,000 and also comes with a fishing lake, swimming pool, steam room, hot-tub and jacuzzi.

Current owner Lisa Mitchell, 48, said of the cave: "This has been a wonderful addition to the home and is great for both entertaining and relaxing quietly.

“It has a range-style cooker and American-style fridge-freezer so you can prepare meals and serve drinks and snacks.

"There is also a fully integrated music system.

"We bought the house when it was only about a year old and were the first people to live there.

"We bought it knowing we would complete it with interior features and décor of our own choosing.

"It is a perfect family home. It is very comfortable and light throughout, with plenty of space to spread out.

"We loved the house itself and the area because it was so private. We also bought some extra paddock land so we could extend the garden."

The property is being sold by Nottingham-based estate agents Chewton Rose which is asking for offers in excess of £650,000.
