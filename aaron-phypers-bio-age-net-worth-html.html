Aaron Phypers Bio: Age To Married Twice | Net Worth Insight To Job

Aaron Phypers features on Real Housewives Of Beverley Hills (RHOBH) alongside his wife Denise Richards. He has appeared in shows such as The Leap (2011) Air Emergency (2009),  and The Curiosity of Penny Parker (2009).

Brief Bio Of Aaron Phypers 

Aaron Phypers was born in Los Angeles, California on September 16, 1972. The American actor stands at a height of 6 feet 2 inches (1.88 m).

A graduate of California State University, Phypers has two siblings: sister, Lynne Phypers, and younger brother, Kyle Phypers.

Wedding Details: Denise Richards is Phypers' Second Wife

On September 8, 2018, Aaron Phypers and Denise Richards shocked the internet as they tied the knot in Malibu. At the time of the wedding, Phypers was 46 years old while Richards was 47 years old.

The couple had a glorious beach wedding. The most beautiful moment of the wedding came when the couple was pronounced husband and wife, which was commemorated by the couple with a long kiss.

Kiss The Bride: Aaron Phypers & Denise Richards Say 'yes' with a long-steamy kiss on their Wedding day (Photo: People.com)

Walking down the aisle to Metallica's iconic song, 'The Unforgiven (Nothing Else Matters)', Richards looked quite ravishing in a gorgeous strapless wedding mini dress with a flowing overlay long tulle skirt, which designer Mark Zunino made in just one day notice. In contrast, Phypers dressed casually in dark jeans and a white button-down shirt.

Meanwhile, the guests at the extravagant wedding included the likes of Rebecca Romijn and Jerry O'Connell, and Richards' fellow Housewives stars Lisa Vanderpump and Camille Grammer. 

Overwhelmed after the wedding, Richards told in a statement to Bravo's The Daily Dish,

I am so happy to officially be married to the love of my life. I can’t wait for everyone to go on this incredible journey with me this season on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. It’s been a wild ride so far!

Know Aaron Phypers and Denise Richards Dating Timeline

The present-day husband and wife, Phypers and Richards, were first spotted spending time together in September 2017.

According to a statement by Denise that she made during an episode of the Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills in February 2019, Richards revealed that she met Aaron at his healing center, Quantum 360 in 2017. She used to visit the healing center for her treatment of anti-aging and DNA repair. 

Phypers and His New Wife Were Both Previously Married

Phypers got married to Desperate Housewives actress Nicollette Sheridan in December 2015.  According to People, their marriage could only sustain for six months. 

Sheridan filed for divorce on June 19, 2016, citing irreconcilable differences. The divorce was finalized in 2018.  

Similarly, Richards was also married to American actor, Charlie Sheen, from June 2002 to November 2006. The divorced couple share two children: Sam J. Sheen and Lola Rose Sheen. In addition to that, the RHOBH star also has another girl, Eloise Joni Richards, whom she adopted in 2011.
