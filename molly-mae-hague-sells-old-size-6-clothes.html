Molly-Mae Hague sells off her old size 6 clothes and admits she's shocked at how skinny she was

MOLLY-MAE Hague has revealed that she's flogging her old size 6 clothes and said she's shocked at how skinny she was.

The 21-year-old showed her fans a pile of clothes including leather trousers that she no longer fits into in her latest YouTube video.

The Love Island star shared a video of herself de-cluttering the huge walk-in wardrobe at her Cheshire home.

Molly-Mae told her followers that she was stunned that she was once a size 6 while sorting through her clothes.

Speaking on her YouTube channel, she said: "To be honest I don't think I can even fit my baby toe into these anymore, they are like a size 6.

"They will never fit me anymore."

The social media influencer said that she plans to sell items, some that have never been worn and still with the tags on.

She said: "I've actually started the process of decluttering my wardrobe. This is going to be a mammoth job and I thought I might as well film it.

"Clothes is my job so it's a massive part of my job. I have accumulated a disgusting amount of clothes since coming out of Love Island.

"I hoarded clothes before that, I think it would be weird if I wasn't obsessed with clothes as an influencer, so I'm not going to feel too bad about the accumulation."

The reality star revealed she's been inundated with offers from fans wishing to purchase her clothes - but Molly is yet to decide how she will sell the items.

The blonde beauty said: "I don't know how to go about it. Whether I set up a Depop and give the money to charity.

"I think it's absolutely ridiculous, one person does not need this much stuff especially when I live my life in joggers and a sweatshirt and a hoodie."

The TV star regularly gives her fans a glimpse of her incredible figure on social media.

At the start of the year Molly-Mae revealed her weekly healthy meal plan as she launched a health kick.

The influencer was cruelly trolled over her figure last summer while on holiday with her boyfriend Tommy Fury, and has since changed up her lifestyle.

Molly announced weeks after the trip that she had started a strict diet.

After a relaxing Christmas at the couple's home in Manchester, Molly is now knuckling down on her nutrition once again.

In August Molly was left angry and upset after mean keyboard warriors criticised her bikini pictures.

In a vlog post not long afterwards, Molly told how she was giving her lifestyle an overhaul - but insisted it had nothing to do with the trolls.

She said: "I’m on a really strict diet plan at the moment.

"For breakfast I’m going to be having some cereal. I’ve been having the same breakfast for the last month. It is 30 grams of Coco Pops – an exciting breakfast."

Making light of the tiny portion, she said: "That’s like a normal portion guys. I must have been having about 300 grams before I knew 30 grams was a normal portion."

Molly wanted to return to the shape she was in pre-fame and admitted she's been finding it hard looking after her body in the year since she was on Love Island.

She said: "I’m on this diet plan that’s been given to me by this really great guy that specialises in body transformations and I’m just really trying to get back to my pre-Love Island body.
