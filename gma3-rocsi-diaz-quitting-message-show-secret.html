ROCSI Diaz has left fans stunned after sharing an inspirational message about never quitting as she reveals a behind-the-scenes secret.

The GMA3 newbie posted on video on Instagram of her reading from a teleprompter on set as a message from Kayne West played in the background.

The message said: "You don't start things and not finished them. You don't quit.

"There's nothing that comes out of quitting. Besides knowing that you didn't finish. We finish everything.

"You started it, finish it. If you going to do it. Do it to try to be the best.

"Not be better than other people. Be the best for you."

"The secret to mastering the teleprompter is reading out loud as much as possible," she wrote in the video caption.

Rocsi also gave some kind words to the people on the GMA3 set.

"I love each time I get a chance to hang out @abcgma3," she wrote.
