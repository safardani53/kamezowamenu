Career

Ivan’s career began in 2014, allowing him to break into the sports spotlight when he finished second in the Vault category at the 2014 Olympic Hopes Russia event. He didn’t stop there, finishing first in the Vault category at Olympic Hopes Russia in 2015.

Earlier this year, he competed in the Russian Junior all-around and Floor Champion categories, winning silver medals on the horizontal bar. Moving on to his international career, he finished second in the All-Around category at the Euro Youth Olympic Festival.

Mr. Ivan has represented his club in numerous national championships, winning gold medals in the majority of them.

He recently competed in the Doha World Cup International Championship in 2022. He competed in the Parallel Bars category for his country and won a bronze medal.

It’s the same championship from which he was banned for a long time after winning and tapping the ‘Z’ symbol on his chest.
