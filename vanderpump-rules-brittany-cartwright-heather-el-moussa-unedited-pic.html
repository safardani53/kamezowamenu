Vanderpump Rules' Brittany Cartwright &amp; Selling Sunset's Heather El Moussa flaunt post-baby bodies i

VANDERPUMP Rules star Brittany Cartwright and Heather El Moussa have shown off their post-baby bikini bodies during a photoshoot.

The pair teamed up with Chanel Iman, who showed off her growing baby bump as they modeled.

Brittany, 34, chose a sensible mauve two-piece swimsuit for the outing in Playa Del Rey, California.

She posed alongside Heather, 35, who wore a yellow bikini.

The ladies were seen walking around the sandy beach.

They also posed beneath a large yellow and white striped umbrella.

The sighting comes amid rumors that Brittany and her husband Jax Taylor will be returning to Vanderpump Rules.

The couple appeared together on Watch What Happens Live to discuss the cheating scandal between Tom Sandoval and Raquel Leviss that rocked viewers and the cast.

They also landed a watch-along special of their own which shows them reacting in real-time to recent episodes of Vanderpump Rules.

Brittany and Jax haven't addressed the rumors regarding their return.

BACK AGAIN?

Bravo fans believe Lala Kent, who currently appears on Vanderpump Rules, accidentally leaked Jax and Brittany's return to the show.

The Give Them Lala host dropped a potential bombshell during an episode of her podcast released in late April as viewers expect a big cast shakeup for next season after the Scandoval affair.

Brittany and Jax were fired from the show in December 2020.

Lala plugged Jax and Brittany's watch-along episodes on an episode of her podcast and seemed to slip a telling detail about the show's future.

“By the way, if you guys haven’t watched the Vanderpump Rules rewatch with Brittany and Jax Taylor, it’s so good.

"To hear their commentary and now that they have an outside perspective since they weren’t really in it. Even during some of my scenes they’re like ‘oh no, Lala no!’ 

"And I’m looking at them like ‘I’m coming for you next season!' Oh, wait…’” Lala says before she takes an awkward pause of silence, perhaps realizing her slip-up. 

Listeners think she mistakenly dropped a massive secret about Jax and Brittany's Bravo fate during the episode.

Bravo fans took to Reddit to share their thoughts on a threat titled: "Jax and Brittany coming back?"

One commenter speculated: "I wouldn't be surprised. Their watch-along show feels like kind of a temperature test."

Someone else chimed in: "Unpopular opinion alert: I'm all for Jax coming back so he and Tom can go at each other."

Another commenter suggested: "I just heard it as legitimate that's what went through her head for a split second and then she remembered they're no longer on the show. 

"That happens to me with my best friend who passed. I still think, 'Oh, I should call him. He'd love this story.' Then I quickly remember."

SPEAKING OUT

Brittany and Jax appeared on Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen, during which time they were asked if they "had any suspicion" about Tom and Raquel Leviss' affair prior to news breaking.

Brittany initially said she did not but quickly walked back on those comments.

She said: "There were times whenever we went to certain events when they came together and I was like, 'This is kind of odd.'

"But I figured Tom Sandoval makes friends, like, really fast with different people."

She concluded: "So I was like maybe they're just close friends right now."

Brittany also said that after the news broke she started "picking up all the Easter eggs."

Jax also said during the show: "Last time we saw them was at our friends' house in Orange County and they were both together."

He said that Ariana Madix – Tom's longtime girlfriend – called Tom during the party to tell him that her "grandmother passed away or got sick or something" and he continued partying.

Jax told host Andy Cohen: "He didn't leave. He used the fact that he couldn't get an Uber. Meanwhile, people were coming in and out of the house all day long."

He added that he "didn't understand it in the beginning," but the circumstances "make sense" knowing what he knows now.

In 2021, a few months after Jax and Brittany were fired from the show, it returned with a new season.

Viewers quickly grew bored with the show and didn't hold back online.

Several fans bashed the show as "boring" during the ninth season, which aired after Jax, Brittany, Stassi Schroeder, and Kristen Doute were all fired.
