A WOMAN has shown off her tiny caravan that she's spent "more days away in than at home" this year.

The micro touring caravan, which is a 2017 Going Go Pod Plus, is just over four metres in length and weighs 580kg - but despite its size is packed full of appliances.

YouTuber Roseanna Sunley, who travels across the UK and works remotely in her mini camper, says she bought the caravan "in a private sale at the beginning of 2023" and often stays in it with her little dog Winston.

In a thorough tour, Roseanna shows off the numerous appliances inside - including a mini stove, sink and dining area, that can be folded down into a bedroom.

It even comes with a toilet that's supposed to slide in and out of the caravan on little hinges, but Roseanna says she "doesn't like the toilet being inside the Pod", so it's kept outside in a small tent.

The caravan also boasts electric and heating controls, USB plugs, a small chest fridge for keeping food, storage cupboards, lights and a pop-top with solar panels.
