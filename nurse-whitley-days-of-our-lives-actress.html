Who plays Nurse Whitley on Days of Our Lives?

DAYS of Our Lives fans were introduced to a new character in May 2023.

The show welcomed Nurse Whitley on May 31, 2023, and now fans want to know why she looks so familiar.

Who plays Nurse Whitley on Days of Our Lives?

Nurse Whitley made her debut at the bedside of Colin Bedford (Jasper Newman) after he was hospitalized for having a brain tumor.

She was seen scolding him for hurting Mayor Abe Carver (James Reynolds) and was urging him to "repent his sins," according to Soaps In Depth.

Whitley's appearance in the episode was brief, but it was long enough for fans to figure out that the role is being played by beloved actress Kim Coles.

Who is Kim Coles?

Kim Coles is a popular actress, comedian, and game show host, who was born on January 11, 1962, in Brooklyn, New York.

She first rose to fame in 1990 as a cast member on the first season of In Living Color but it was arguably her role as Synclaire James-Jones on Living Single that put her on the map.

Additionally, Coles has starred in Frasier, The Geena Davis Show, and One on One.

Some fans might also recognize her as the former host of BET's game show Pay It Off.

After Nurse Whitley's debut on the show, Coles took to Instagram to express her excitement about being a part of the soap opera franchise, revealing that she has been a fan of the show since her youth.

How can I watch Days of Our Lives?

For over 55 years, one of the longest-running scripted television programs in the world aired live on weekdays on NBC.

However, in September 2022, it was announced that DOOL would be leaving its usual timeslot and heading to streaming.

The show can now be watched exclusively on Peacock with an active subscription.
