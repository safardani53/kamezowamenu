Who is Jacob Sartorius?

Jacob Sartorius was born in Tulsa, Oklahoma on October 2, 2002.

He is an internet star, who rose to fame by posting lip-sync videos on Vine and Musical.ly.

In 2016, Sartorius released his debut single, Sweatshirt, followed by his EP, The Last Text, in 2017 - for which he went on a world tour that included seven countries.

He was the ninth most searched musical artist of 2016 and as of October 2022 has over 10million followers on Instagram where he regularly posts pictures of both work and his personal life.

In 2016 and 2017, Sartorius was nominated for a Teen Choice Award.

Did Jacob Sartorius date Millie Bobby Brown?

In February 2019, Jacob posted a video on YouTube announcing he was no longer in a relationship.

He said: "I'm single, like actually, I don't think you guys know that. I've seen so many posts. I actually am single. I'm not joking."

It's not known when the couple split up, but Jacob was first linked to Stranger Things actress, Millie Bobby Brown, back in October 2017.

According to US Weekly, the pair had been on holiday together and even celebrated New Year's Eve with her family at Walt Disney World in Florida.

Brown confirmed they were dating on Valentine's Day 2018 as she shared a picture of them with their foreheads together.

Sartorius told Millie he loved her as he wished her a happy 14th birthday, writing: “Happy birthday to this beautiful girl, 14!! you’re the funniest, most caring person in the entire world.

“I love you and miss you so much!!

"Everybody go wish this girl a happy birthday @milliebobbybrown.”

In April 2018, however, rumours circulated that Sartorius had asked another girl to send him nude photos of herself via Snapchat.

Following the news, Brown reportedly deleted all of their photos together on her Instagram.

The couple called things off for several weeks before reconciling by the month's end, according to Girlfriend.

In January 2019 it was rumoured they were still a couple after they were both spotted wearing the same T-shirt, however, Sartorius put a swift end to the dating speculation.
