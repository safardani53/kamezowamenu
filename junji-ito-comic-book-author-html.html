Comic Book Author Junji Ito was born in Japan on July 13, 1963. He's 60 years old today.

He is most recognized for his contributions to the Japanese comic books Tomie, Gyo, and Uzumaki as a writer and manga artist. His comic book Tomie, as well as several of his other works, was converted into films and television shows.
