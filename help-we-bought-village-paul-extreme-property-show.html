Help! We Bought A Village viewers all say the same thing about most extreme property show yet

THOSE who tuned into Help! We Bought A Village were all saying the same thing about the colossal project.

Paul Mappley and Yip Ward, both 48, were living in a mobile home in Tunbridge Wells, Kent, when they decided to make a huge leap and purchase a home abroad.

The couple decided to buy hamlet La Busliere, in Normandy, France, and their journey is being documented by Channel 4.

Paul and Yip's new property includes six cottages, two barns, a two story workshop, open barn and paddock, as well as a stone well, cider press and communal bread oven.

The pair, who are both landscape gardeners, are planning to turn the historic hamlet into luxury holiday accommodation.
