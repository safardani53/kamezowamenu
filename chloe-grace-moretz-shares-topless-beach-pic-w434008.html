Chloe Grace Moretz Shares Topless Beach Pic

https://www.instagram.com/p/BI8vFz8gmED/

Young love! Chloe Grace Moretz shared a sultry (but totally SFW) topless Instagram photo from her day at the beach, taken by her boyfriend, Brooklyn Beckham. 

The 19-year-old actress posed for a tasteful shot, in which she’s laying on a blanket with her bare back to the camera, and it appears she ditched her bikini top.

“Laguna (photo credit @brookynbeckham),” she captioned the black-and-white pic by her photographer beau on Wednesday, August 10.

In another snap shared by Beckham on Wednesday, the 5th Wave actress smiles as she walks along the water. She rocks a bikini and covers up with an open button-down shirt in this photo.

Beach day ❤️

A post shared by bb (@brooklynbeckham) on Aug 10, 2016 at 9:40am PDT

“Beach day,” the 17-year-old Brit wrote of the Instagram, along with a heart emoji.

The pair have been dating on and off since they met at an L.A. SoulCycle class in 2014, but they only officially confirmed their relationship in May. That same month, they made their red carpet debut as a couple at the L.A. premiere for Moretz’s film Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising.

“We’re in a relationship, it’s fine, it’s no biggie,” she told Andy Cohen on Watch What Happens Live. “I think the more I don’t make it mysterious the more people don’t care, so yes, we’re in a relationship.” 

Luckily, Brooklyn’s famous parents, David and Victoria Beckham, seem to approve of Moretz. In July, fashion designer Victoria gave Moretz a cute shout-out on Instagram after the actress’ speech at the Democratic National Convention. “That’s what I’m talking about!!!! Girl Power!!!!” the former Spice Girl, 42, captioned a pic of Moretz at the podium.
