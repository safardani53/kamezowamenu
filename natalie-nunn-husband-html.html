Natalie Nunn Husband, Split, Cheating, Daughter, Scotty &amp; Net Worth

Natalie Nunn, born Natalie Tynika Nunn, is a renowned reality TV star-turned-model, fashion entrepreneur, and Onlyfans content creator best known for her appearance in the record-breaking fourth season of Oxygen's Bad Girls Club in 2009.

She subsequently went on to star in several reality TV shows, including spinoffs of the Oxygen hit series in season 13, Half Battle Spectacular, Love Games: Bad Girls Need Love Too, Bad  Girls All-Star Battle,  Celebrity Big Brother, and Baddies ( Bad Girls reunion show created by her).

And of course, Natalie has always been into feuds and controversies. Her latest feud was with fellow cast member Scotty happened after Natalie snapped at her. The controversy soon went viral like wildfire on Reddit and other platforms. 

As per Natalie, she alleged that Scotty didn't defend her when Chrisean Rock slapped her but chose to "smile" at the situation. 

In addition, back in February 2023, Nunn had beef with Tommie Lee after the latter allegedly disrespected Chris Brown and his son after Natalie brought her backstage. The feud happened ahead of their boxing match produced by Zeus Network.

Later, Nunn won the match couple of days later, dominating her opponent the entire event.

 And in her personal life, the 38-year-old star is also a married woman and has a kid. She has come far from her time in Bad Girls Club when she often boasted about being a free soul, unmarried and without children. 

If you don't know, the Concord native has been married to her husband, Jacob Payne, since May 2012. And together, they share a daughter, Journey Ruth Payne, born on April 26, 2017.

Over the years, Nunn's personal life has gone through a topsy-turvy emotional journey due to various reasons, including getting married, suffering a miscarriage, and eventually becoming a mother.

Natalie Nunn and Her husband Jacob Payne Met at a Club

Before proceeding into the fashion entrepreneur's emotional journey of becoming a mom, let's dive into the details of how her love story with her husband kick-started.

Nunn and Payne reportedly first met each other while making an appearance at a club in Detroit in 2011. During a 2012 interview, she recalled spotting Payne at the club with some of his friends and falling in love with him at first sight.

"He was so charming and handsome, I fell in love at first sight. He then came to LA to visit me and never went back," she told WeTV.

A few months later, Payne, a football player from Michigan, made the trip down to Los Angeles to visit his then-girlfriend Nunn, and surprisingly, he never went back home. The football player reportedly had his personal things shipped to him in LA, where he began a new life with the reality TV star.

In April 2012, the 1990-born football player went down on his knees and proposed to the Celebrity Big Brother alum during a vacation trip to the island of Jamaica in Negril.

Their wedding followed just a month later, as they tied the knot at a private ceremony that took place at a friend's mansion in Topanga Canyon, California.

Natalie Nunn’s Emotional Journey of Becoming a Mom

The couple went through a great deal after they got married and planned to welcome their first baby. In November 2014, Nunn confirmed that she was pregnant with their first baby.

But in 2015, Nunn was spotted performing a rigorous gym session, which made fans speculate that she had previously faked her pregnancy to gain publicity. However, she was quick to clarify the speculations, as she revealed that she had suffered a painful miscarriage during an interview with Oxygen.

She said that she chose to keep the miscarriage private, and even though the experience was heartbreaking, she revealed that the couple was trying to get pregnant again.

Yes, we are moving forward and doing things differently a second time. We are trying for a due date this year. This time, we're hoping for twins!

Happily for the couple, they became pregnant, and they announced they were expecting a baby girl in November 2016. They eventually welcomed their first child, Journey Ruth, in April next year.

Natalie Nunn was Accused of Cheating

Nunn and Payne hit the rock when the reality star was accused of cheating on her husband in 2019.

Her Celebrity Big Brother co-star Chloe Ayling alleged that she was involved in a threesome with Dan Osbourne (husband of Jacqueline Jossa)  and Nunn, which happened in 2018 after a night of heavy drinking in London.

Things started getting worse when her husband  Payne was spotted moving his things out of their home on December 6, 2019, even though Osbourne and Nunn denied Ayling's claims. Also, Nun was seen hugging Osborne publicly amidst the claims.

Some days later, he was seen moving out of their home and walking around London alone without his wedding ring after which many Tabloids reported that Natalie had been dumped by him for cheating after seven years of their relationship.

That said, the couple apparently sorted out their differences, as they were spotted showing some PDA on the streets of London a week later. Since then, their marriage has apparently gained strength.
