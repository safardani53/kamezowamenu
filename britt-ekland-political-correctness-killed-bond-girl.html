ACTRESS Britt Ekland says political correctness has killed the traditional Bond Girl.

The 77-year-old played Mary Goodnight in 1974’s The Man with the Golden Gun.

She said: “I’m the proudest Bond Girl there is because there are not a lot of us left, and there won’t be any in future.

“The Bond Girl has to look good in a bikini. That was her role.

“You wouldn’t see her in a bikini next to Daniel Craig in a suit today.”

She also told The Guardian how ex-hubby Peter Sellers told the world they were getting wed before proposing.

The actress said: “He said, ‘I’ve told the press we’re getting married. Is that OK with you?’

“I didn’t know what to say. Even to this day, I don’t know what I said.”
