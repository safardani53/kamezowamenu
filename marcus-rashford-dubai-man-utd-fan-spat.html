Marcus Rashford meets boxer Regis Prograis in Dubai as he escapes Manchester for welcome break after

MARCUS RASHFORD made the most of his time off by meeting up with pro boxer Regis Prograis in Dubai.

The school meals hero, 24, is currently enjoying a welcome break in the Middle East.

Manchester United star Rashford was seen chatting with the American former welterweight champion after his win against Tyrone McKenna.

Rashford was overheard in a video clip asking Prograis to tap in his Instagram handle, so he could follow him.

The Man Utd forward is enjoying time away from Manchester after he was subjected to heckling by Red Devils fans following defeat to Atletico Madrid that condemned them to Champions League exit.

The 24-year-old was accused of swearing at the fan after being seen pointing a finger.
