Matt Kuchar Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Matt Kuchar (born Matthew Gregory Kuchar on June 21, 1978 in Winter Park, Florida) is an American professional golfer. He has played on the Nationwide Tour and on the PGA Tour, the latter of which he has won seven times. Kuchar has earned over $30 million in prize money since beginning his professional career in 2000.

Best Known For:
Matt Kuchar is best known for playing golf professionally.

Personal Life:

Kuchar married former tennis player Sybi Parker in 2003, and they currently live in Georgia together. The couple has two sons, Cameron Cole and Carson Wright.
