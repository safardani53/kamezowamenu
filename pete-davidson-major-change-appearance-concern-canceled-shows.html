PETE Davidson has debuted a major change to his appearance while appearing in an ad for Smartwater.

The 28-year-old Saturday Night Live alum showed off his freshly shaven head in the commercial.

Pete chopped off his trademark dark hair for a close buzzcut and fresh shave.

The King of Staten Island actor wore a white button-down top as he appeared in the ad.

Pete's new haircut comes amid concern for the comedian after he was forced to cancel a string of stand-up shows.

He axed a series of gigs in Phoenix, with fans speculating that it was done so he could attend a court hearing for a reckless driving charge.

Fans shared their worries for Pete on Reddit.

One posted: "With all these cancellations I think it’s very important to remember that Pete is a real human being. With physical and mental health issues which he has been more open about than most public figures."

Another added: "I felt the schedule was a bit over-ambitious. Maybe best he focus on one venue at a time.

"It's the first time he has toured in years. Maybe he didn't realize how truly busy he would be. Hope court goes well."

Earlier this week, Stand Up Live, whom Pete was slated to perform for, sent out an announcement about his shows.

The organizer wrote: "We're really sorry but we just heard that Pete has to cancel his shows at Stand Up Live from July 27th-July 30th due to scheduling conflicts."

Full refunds for the canceled shows will be given out.

One fan speculated online: "I have seen some ppl wondering why all the cancellations and I am just assuming but I do believe it has something to do with the crash.

The social media user continued: "He would have had many lawyers appointments, court appearances.

"The one in Phoenix I knew would be canceled since his court date is July 27th and I’m sure he would need to appear in California court."

Back in March, Pete and his girlfriend Chase Sui-Wonders, 27, were involved in a frightening car accident.

The Suicide Squad actor was reportedly behind the wheel of the Mercedes car, as first reported by TMZ.

Allegedly, Pete was speeding when he lost control just before midnight.

During the car ride, the vehicle hopped over a curb and ran over a fire hydrant.

The vehicle eventually slammed into the side of a nearby house in Los Angeles.

'DEVASTATING'

The Los Angeles County District Attorney's Office charged the comedian with one count of reckless driving, according to TMZ.

A spokesperson for the district attorney's office told the outlet: "Luckily, no one was seriously injured as a result of this collision.

"We know that reckless driving can have devastating consequences. In 2022, traffic fatalities in Los Angeles have reached the highest levels seen in 20 years.

"This is an alarming trend that we cannot ignore. As a result, it's crucial that we take all allegations of reckless driving seriously and hold those responsible accountable."
