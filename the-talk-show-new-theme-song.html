The Talk fans rip show's 'awful' new theme song after tune's debut on season 13 premiere &amp; beg for o

THE Talk's new theme song has been ripped by viewers as loyal fans are begging for the program to bring back the old tune.

The song, titled Good Company (The Talk Theme), premiered on September 12 for The Talk's 13th season.

Andy Grammer- well known for his hit song Honey, I'm Good- performed the theme song, which has a similar upbeat tempo to his hit.

The Talk posted a video of the song's premiere and captioned it: "Nothing like starting off Season 13 with a NEW theme song by the talented Andy Grammer!"

The show's hosts Akbar Gbajabiamila, Amanda Kloots, Jerry O'Connell,
Sheryl Underwood, and Natalie Morales danced around the stage with huge smiles on their faces while the song played.

While the hosts of the show feel like they're in good company with their co-hosts, their fans on Facebook weren't kind to the new theme. song.

TALK HISTORY

The theme song chaos isn't the only drama from the show as it comes after Sharon Osbourne was fired from the program in March of 2021.

This occurred after she had an on-air fight with current co-host Sheryl.

In Bethenny Frankel's podcast, Just B, Sharon said she was angry at CBS for the way she was let go from the show.

She told Bethenny: "I can honestly say - now I hold no hostilities against anything that was done to me, except for CBS, and I will never forgive that network."

The Talk hit another rough patch after beloved executive producer Heather Gray died at the young age of 50.

Heather was at the show for 11 years until she passed in July.

The previous season of the show ended a week early because of her passing.
