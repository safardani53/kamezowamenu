Welp, that definitely didn’t happen!

Big Sean: Why He Apologized To Naya Rivera After Ariana Grande Split

Sean may be ignoring Ariana, but the break up has caused him to turn to another ex, Naya Rivera, despite the nasty break up they endured in 2014.

Unfortunately for the rapper, Naya doesn’t seem too interested in talking to him right now — after all, she is recently married with a baby on the way!
