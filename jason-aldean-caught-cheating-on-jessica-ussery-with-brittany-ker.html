Jason Aldean Caught Cheating on Jessica Ussery With Brittany Kerr, Apologizes

Country singer Jason Aldean was caught by a celebrity gossip photographer getting close with someone who was not his wife, Jessica Ussery, over the weekend.

He’s already come clean about the cheating scandal and apologized.

“Hey Guys – I wanted to talk to you directly, so you’re hearing the truth from me and not just reading allegations made about my personal life on gossip web sites,” Aldean wrote on Facebook.

 

“The truth is that I screwed up. I had too much to drink, let the party get out of hand and I acted inappropriately at a bar.”

“I left alone, caught the bus to our next show and that’s the end of the story. I ultimately ended up embarrassing my family and myself.”

“I’m not perfect, and I’m sorry for disappointing you guys. I really appreciate being able to work through this privately with my family and for all your continued support.”

The woman the country star was caught smooching, according to TMZ, is former American Idol contestant Brittany Kerr.

Aldean, who performed this weekend in Southern California for a music festival, married Jessica Ussery in 2001 and they have two children together.
