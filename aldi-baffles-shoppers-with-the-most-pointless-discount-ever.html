Aldi baffles shoppers with the most pointless discount ever

A BIZZARE discount of just 1p off a packet of biscuits has been spotted at an Aldi store.

The supermarket, famous for its bargains, has slashed the price of its Mcvities Victoria biscuits selection from £4 to £3.99.

The deal was on offer at a branch in Boston, Lincolnshire and was spotted by a man during a shopping trip with his mum.

"I only took the picture for a laugh, there's so much bad stuff going on in the world at the moment it doesn't hurt to make people laugh once in a while,” he told Lincolnshirelive.

The website also reported that Lincoln shopper Lenka Garstang and her daughter Ellie spotted a similar reduction - a pot of olives down from £4.50 to £4.49 - earlier this year.

“It’s a ridiculous thing to do. Why would you even bother,” she said.
