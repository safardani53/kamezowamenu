Im a mom my $25 Amazon buy will make storing your kids artwork so much easier

THANKS to one mom's Amazon find, you can clear off the fridge and start hanging kids' creations in style.

Plus, if you have a super-creative child, you don't need to find additional storage space for their art thanks to a sneaky compartment.

Parenting influencer Jordan Reeh shared the affordable tip on TikTok, and other parents flocked to Amazon afterward to buy up the must-have tool.

"Don't know what to do with the plethora of art you get from your kids? Reeh asked at the start of the clip. "I have a hack for that."

She held up a piece of art made by one of her children, then headed over to a gallery wall featuring similar drawings.

"These Amazon frames open from the front so all you have to do is push new artwork in," she explained. The frames start at $25 and come in a variety of colors.

The hinged covers on the picture frames lifted easily, and Reeh was able to slide new artwork in without any hassle.

Plus, the newest pieces went right on top of the existing artwork thanks to the clever, built-in storage solution.

"Each frame holds up to 50 pieces," Reeh said, closing up the frame and displaying her updated gallery wall.

Try using the frames wherever your kids work on their art. "They're great in the mudroom or playroom," she said.
