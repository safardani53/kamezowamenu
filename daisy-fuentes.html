Daisy Fuentes Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Daisy Fuentes (born on November 17, 1966 in Havana, Cuba) is a Cuban-born American television host and model. She was raised in Madrid, Spain. She got her start as MTV’s first Latina VJ in 1993. One year later, she began hosting her own talk show, Daisy, on CNBC. She was also the host of House of Style. She co-hosted America’s Funniest Home Videos with John Fugelsang from 1998 to 1999. Over the years, she’s also hosted the ALMA Awards, the Billboard Latin Awards, Miss USA, Miss Universe and Dick Clark’s New Year’s Rockin’ Eve. She also became Revlon’s first Latina spokeswoman. Daisy has launched a clothing line, hair care line, fragrances and more. She is currently the co-presenter of La Voz Kids, the Spanish version of The Voice with just kids. Daisy married Richard Marx on Dec. 25, 2015.

Best Known For:

Daisy is best known for becoming MTV’s first Latina VJ. She’s also recognized for her hosting duties over the years, and her clothing line, hair care line and fragrances are beloved by many.

Personal Life:

Daisy was married to actor Timothy Adams from 1991 to 1995. She dated Luis Miguel for three years before breaking up in 1998. She also became engaged to singer Matt Goss in 2003. They eventually broke up. She married singer Richard Marx on Dec. 23, 2015.
