How many kids does Michelle Yeoh have?

MICHELLE Yeoh has had her share of relationships while in the spotlight.

However, fans of the Marvel alum are curious to know if she's had any children.

How many kids does Michelle Yeoh have?

Although Michelle Yeoh does not have any children of her own, she has one stepson named Nicolas with her husband Jean Todt.

The Shang-Chi actress revealed she and Jean made attempts to have children but were unsuccessful.

Michelle explained to YOU Magazine: "I tried IVF, everything. I was desperate.

"I love kids and saw myself surrounded by them but there’s only so much you can put your body and mind through.

"It comes to a stage where you have to accept it, move on, and deal with the repercussions.

"In a Chinese family, [having children] means a great deal."

The Golden Globe Award winner added: "I have always wanted my own children.

"Naturally, you will feel disheartened when things do not go your way. Maybe it's just my fate."

Michelle finally tied the knot with Jean Todt, ex-Ferrari CEO, after 19 years.

Why did Michelle divorce her first husband?

From 1988 to 1992, Michelle was married to luxury goods businessman Dickson Poon.

Ultimately, she was the one to pull the plug on her marriage to Dickson due to her inability to conceive children.

Similar to the infertility problems she experienced with Jean Todt later in her life, she first navigated those same issues with her ex-husband.

Michelle confessed to Bustle: "As it turned out, I was unable to have children and if I’m being honest with myself, I didn’t want for us to be bitter 10 years down the road.

"In Asian families, people want to have sons and daughters because they are an extension and a legacy.

"When you have the realization that you can’t have kids, you have to deal with it.

"You deal with the curveballs that are thrown at you. I really, really wanted to start a family."
