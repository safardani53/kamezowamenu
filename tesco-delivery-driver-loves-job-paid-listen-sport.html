Im a Tesco delivery driver and my jobs the best - Im basically paid to listen to sport all

A TESCO delivery driver has insisted he loves every second of his job - especially being able to listen to TalkSport all day.

Scott took to TikTok to wax lyrical about his position, captioning the video: "Love me job."

In the clip, he documented a "day in the life of a Tesco delivery driver", beginning by loading up crates of shopping into the back of his truck.

"TalkSport ready," he wrote over the clip, as he showed his radio tuned in to the sports radio station.

Scott was then seen unloading some crates from the van and delivering the shopping, checking customers off his list as he went.

"Don’t mind a bit of rain," he wrote, as the weather worsened, before admitting that he "might be lost".

After lunchtime, another reload and more deliveries, Scott's day came to an end as he returned the van to the store and locked up, before heading home.

But Scott hit back, responding: "Nah I’m chilled because I love the job!"

"Better than sitting in an office with people that you don’t like! Looking for a van driving job myself soon," someone else added.
