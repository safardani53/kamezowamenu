Actress Agnes Bruckner Cast as Anna Nicole Smith in Lifetime Movie

Lifetime has found their Anna Nicole!

Private Practice actress Agnes Bruckner has been tapped to appear as late Playboy model Anna Nicole Smith in the network's upcoming made-for-TV movie The Anna Nicole Story, Deadline reports.

Bruckner, 27, will likely find at least one thing in common with Smith, whose early life as Vicky Lynn Hogan will be chronicled in the film. Growing up as a child model, Bruckner appeared in a beauty pageant at the urging of her mother when she was 8 years old.

Transitioning to acting at age 11, Bruckner went on to appear on television shows including The Bold and the Beautiful. She made her feature film debut as a high school student involved in an affair with her teacher in 2002's Blue Car. Bruckner was also featured in The Glass House and Murder By Numbers, costarring Sandra Bullock.
