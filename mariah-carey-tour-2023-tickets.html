Mariah Carey tour 2023: How can I get tickets?

MARIAH Carey is embarking on her 2023 Merry Christmas One and All! tour.

Now, fans want to know how they can purchase tickets.

How can I get tickets to Mariah Carey's tour?

You can get tickets to Mariah Carey's Merry Christmas One and All! tour through Live Nation via Ticketmaster.

Tickets first went on sale on Friday, October 6, 2023.

On Tuesday, October 17, a presale took place after Carey added a date, with the general on-sale happening on Friday, October 20.

Another sale for two additional dates began on Friday, November 3 at 9 am.

How can I listen to Mariah Carey's Christmas music?

You can listen to Carey's Christmas music via various streaming platforms — including Apple Music and Spotify — as well as through YouTube.

The pop star has recorded two Christmas albums: 1994's Merry Christmas and 2010's Merry Christmas II You, which include the massively successful track, All I Want for Christmas Is You.

In 2019, Carey released a Deluxe Anniversary Edition of Merry Christmas.

You can also listen to the megastar's holiday hits on the radio, as various Christmas stations will likely play All I Want for Christmas Is You on repeat.
