Career

Mr. Kishor Das, a former IT engineer, changed his professional path to acting and modeling after winning modeling contests. Kishor Das dabbled with modeling while working as an IT engineer in 2015. He took part in model searches and fashion shows. In addition to this, he has already appeared in short films. He has attempted to win reality series such as Dance India Dance and India’s Got Talent but was unsuccessful.
