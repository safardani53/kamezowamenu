S Club 7 confirm huge reunion tour to celebrate 25th anniversary

S CLUB 7 have confirmed they are reuniting for a 25th anniversary tour across the UK and Ireland.

All seven members - Bradley McIntosh, Hannah Spearritt, Jo O’Meara, Jon Lee, Paul Cattermole, Rachel Stevens and Tina Barrett - will get back together for the S Club 7 Reunited tour in October.

They announced the news on The One Show and revealed tickets would be going on sale at 10am this Friday through Ticketmaster.

They will kick off the tour in Liverpool’s M&S Bank Arena on October 13 and play a total of 11 dates across the country before ending at London’s O2 Arena on October 28.

They said: "After eight years it feels amazing to announce that we're reuniting and performing together again.

“We're so excited to bring the S Club Party back to our fans across the country to celebrate 25 years of S Club 7.

“We can't even believe it's been so long! Music and friendship have always been at the core of everything that we've ever done. We hope everyone can join us for one big party!!"

Jo, Rachel and Tina sent fans wild earlier this week when they attended the Brit Awards together.

During their heyday, all 11 of their singles hit the top five and they had four No1s including Never Had A Dream Come True and Don’t Stop Movin’.

They also had a string of hit TV series’, a movie called Seeing Double and their own talent show.

They split up in 2003 and then reunited in 2014, later going on an arena tour the following year.

Last month, Jo told how she had undergone several major operations after a horror back injury which left her feeling like she had been shot.

But she is now on the road to recovery ahead of the live shows.

Hannah recently told The Sun how she was left homeless over Christmas after her landlord sold her family home.
