Miley Cyrus Sexiest Looks: Photos Hollywood Life

Miley Cyrus’ edgy, sexy style is unmatched — although, she didn’t start experimenting with sultry bodysuits or revealing looks until she shed her Disney Channel identity. Despite having a superstar country-singing dad, Miley rose to fame on her own merit, while portraying the title character of the Disney Channel television series Hannah Montana from 2006–2011.

Following the show’s end, Miley admittedly struggled with being able to express her authentic style because of the fan-described ‘good girl’ image Hannah Montana and Disney had cast over her. But, she managed to break away from the sparkles and blonde wig — and carved her own lane in defining what sexy really is.

In a July 2019 interview, Miley told ELLE magazine that she cut ties with her squeaky-clean Disney image ‘once I was 18 because it felt ridiculous.’ She admitted, “The minute I had s**, I was kind of like, I can’t put the f****** wig on again. It got weird. I grew up.”

She went on to compare her transformation to an image of an actor playing Peter Pan smoking backstage at Disneyland (which, the mag noted, she actually did see). ‘And I was like, ‘That’s me. That’s the kind of dreams I’m crushing,’ Miley said, adding, ‘I’m not a Disney mascot. I’m a person.’

Miley’s style certainly proves it. She smoldered at the Tom Ford show at Milk Studios during NYFW Fall/Winter 2020 on Feb. 7. Look at that pixie cut!
