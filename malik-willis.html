Malik Willis: Career

As mentioned earlier, Malik has been playing football from an early age. He was also part of his high school and college football teams.

College Career

Malik was not given enough playtime as a junior at three-star college prospect Roswell. But gradually, his playtime increased, and he performed well.

Willis was also ranked as the #35 best high school player in Georgia. Apart from that, 247Sports Composite, which happened to be a reputed news portal in the sports world, ranked him as the #368 best player in the 2017 high school graduating class.

Likewise, before moving to Auburn University, Willis made a record of 2,562 yards and ran for 1,033 yards, totaling 37 touchdowns.

Then, he got admission and started playing for Auburn’s team, where he played fifteen games. In two years, he made a record of 11 of 14 throws for 69 yards with a score and 309 yards rushing with two touchdowns. But in 2019, he was again transferred to Liberty University.

However, Willis could not play that season due to some transfer rules, but he got the opportunity to play and instantly was named as Liberty’s starting quarterback.

Before graduating from Liberty, he was awarded prestigious accolades like the Dudley Award, #1 college football player in Virginia, Bobby Bowden Award, MVP of the 2020 Cure Bowl, and Lending Tree Bowl of 2021.

Nonetheless, after getting a degree in the multi-disciplinary subject, he finally graduated from Liberty; hence, his professional career as a professional athlete started there.

Professional Career

Being a part of the NFL is a dream to many, and Malik Willis was no exception. And this dream of him got fulfilled in 2022 in April after he got drafted by the Tennessee Titans.

Willis had shown his exceptional performance in Liberty, so it didn’t take long to grab everyone’s attention. Even though he was considered to be among one of the top picks during NFL presumptions, it did not go like that.

Many teams considered Willis’ style simplistic and chose other players over him. However, at last, he got picked by the Titans anyways. Nonetheless, there still is a long way to go for Malik, and as soon as a significant thing happens in his career, we will update you.
