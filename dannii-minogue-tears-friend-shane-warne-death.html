DANNII Minogue has broken down in tears over the death of her close friend Shane Warne - saying she "misses him so much".

The singing sensation talked about the close friendship she shared with the late cricketer on Australia's Channel Seven tribute show today.

Dannii, 50, opened up about the close relationship she shared with cricket player Shane, who passed away earlier this month.

The star was found unresponsive at a villa in Thailand on March 4, before being pronounced dead at the age of 52.

Today, the Australian broadcaster paid tribute to the cricketer with anecdotes and messages from a number of his friends.

"Shane, I miss you so much. Can't believe you're not here. Mr Sunshine,!" Dannii told the cameras, getting emotional and trying to hold back tears.

Speaking about Shane's charitable side, the star went on to gush over the bowler's love of giving to others, calling him "joyful".

"All these little stories we have... somebody would say, 'My friend's going through a hard time and he's a Warnie fan'," Dannii recalled.

"And he [Shane] would say, 'Just ring him now. Get him on the phone'.
