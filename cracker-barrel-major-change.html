Cracker Barrel makes major change to its meal offerings - and customers will love the options

CUSTOMERS can expect to see four new offerings from Cracker Barrel.

The restaurant chain has added “homestyle family meal and catering options” as the summer starts.

The catering menu includes favorites like the Build Your Own Homestyle Chicken Sandwich Bar with its signature Homestyle Fried Chicken.

Others include the Tenders Trio Platter with a choice of three different chicken tenders: plain, sweet n' smoky maple bacon, and new Kick'n Buffalo Ranch. 

For more information on the options or to place an order, you can visit Cracker Barrel's catering section.

“With all the summer gathering moments approaching, at Cracker Barrel our goal is to offer guests a variety of hassle-free homestyle family meal and catering options to ease the stress of hosting gatherings, whether small or large,” Thomas Yun, Cracker Barrel vice president of culinary innovation, said in a statement.

NEW EATS

Cracker Barrel has added three new items to the catering menu and one fan favorite.

The new eats include the Sirloin Steak Tips Catering Bundle, Barrel Cheeseburger Slider Platter, and the Sirloin Steak Tips n' Tenders Family Meal Basket.

For a catering option, the Sirloin Steak Tips Catering Bundle comes with Sirloin Steak Tips in a garlic butter glaze.

It's served with two or three country sides and a choice of biscuits or corn muffins.

The Barrel Cheeseburger Slider Platter includes 10 mini burgers topped with American cheese and served with ketchup, mayo, mustard and pickles.

The Sirloin Steak Tips n' Tenders Family Meal Basket offers Sirloin Steak Tips, served with a garlic butter glaze, plus 10 hand-breaded fried or grilled chicken tenders.

It also comes with a choice of two country sides, buttermilk biscuits and a choice of dipping sauce.

Customers can add summertime sides and beverages to wash down the hearty eats.

For a limited time, Cracker Barrel has brought back its classic side dish, seasoned corn on the cob.

However, customers can also grab mac n' cheese, coleslaw, and green beans for a catering bundle or a la carte.

Lastly, fans have the option to add a half-gallon of Strawberry Lemonade.

IN THE NEWS

Like many restaurants and retailers, Cracker Barrel has closed up shop in Missouri after nearly 20 years.

The Ferguson location shuttered in March due to the effect of the pandemic and staffing troubles.

The company also said it plans to support all impacted employees, including providing work opportunities at other Cracker Barrel locations in the area. 

However, the company revealed that the store was just the beginning of its closures.

Cracker Barrel announced it would be shutting the doors of all its Portland, Oregon stores. 

“As a standard course of business, we continually evaluate the performance of our stores, using various criteria to ensure we are meeting the needs of our guests and our business,” a spokesperson said at the time.
