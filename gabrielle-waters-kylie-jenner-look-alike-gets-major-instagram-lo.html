Gabrielle Waters: Kylie Jenner Look-Alike Gets Major Instagram Love

There have been multiple cases of obsessive fans who went to great lengths to look just like Kim Kardashian.

Even with surgery and thousands of dollars spent on hair and makeup, they’ve had mixed results (to put it kindly).

What’s remarkable about Gabrielle Waters is that she reportedly didn’t realize that she bears a remarkable resemblance to Kylie Jenner until her social media followers started pointing it out.

Now, the Michigan 19-year-old has devoted herself to mimicking Kylie’s hair, makeup and wardrobe.

She describes Kylie as her “idol,” and in some pics she’s really a dead-ringer for the 18-year-old Instagram queen.

Now, the question is: As Kylie begins to look more like Kim, will Gabrielle follow suit?

We hope not. The world has enough Kim doppelgangers as it is.
