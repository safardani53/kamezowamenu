Peter Andre blown away as daughter Princess, 13, masters horseriding as family trot into the sunset

PETER Andre was blown away by his daughter's horseriding skills as the family trotted around Cyprus on their latest holiday.

The 47-year-old singer is on the sun-drenched island spending half term with Junior, 15, and Princess, 13, his children with ex Katie Price.

They all went for a horse ride yesterday with his brothers Danny, Michael and Chris, and Peter was staggered by just how skilled his eldest daughter was.

As she managed to turn a corner just by looking to one side, he used her family nickname, telling her: "Amazing, Bista."

He filmed Princess as she bobbed about on horseback writing: "Awesome 😎. Proud papa."

However, he added the disclaimer: "Admittedly I didn’t teach her 😊🐴."

Indeed, the horseriding skills most likely came courtesy of Pete's ex Katie, who has long been obsessed with riding.

One of Peter's followers alluded to that, writing: "Awwww loves horses just like her mommy 👌🏻😊."

The family were later pictured lined up on horseback with the sun going down behind them, in the manner of an old Western film.

Pete captioned the snap, which was taken beside the twinkling blue sea: "This was special :))."

He tagged his three brothers into the picture and used the hashtag "#homefromhome".

The Mysterious Girl star's parents are Greek Cypriots, though he was born in London and they now live in Australia.

They helped him to build a luxurious home on the island nation, complete with a swimming pool and tennis court.

It does not look like Peter's wife Emily, 31, or their children Theo, three, and Amelia, six, made the trip.
