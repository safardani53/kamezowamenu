You can buy a tiny home plan on Etsy for $95 - the 'budget modern cabin' includes high ceilings, two

ETSY is selling a tiny home plan for $95, and the entire budget cabin includes two bedrooms and a loft.

Tiny homes are surging in popularity as Americans look to save money on their monthly expenses.

While traditional houses typically sell for $300,000, tiny homes can be built or purchased for anywhere between $10,000 and $100,000.

On Etsy, several tiny homes have popped up for sale, but there’s now blueprints for you to build the tiny home of your dreams.

The 20x26 Adirondack Cabin plan even includes a lofted bedroom.

Inside the blueprint, the plans show a 10x6 bathroom, 10x9 bedroom and a spacious kitchen and living room combination.

The kitchen space is 12x10 while the living room is a bit larger at 15x10.

It even includes a patio on the outside of the home’s plans.

On top of the kitchen and living room is a lofted space that could work as your bedroom if you converted the blueprints into a real home.

Also included in the set of plans is a building materials list.

WHAT THEY'RE SAYING

Shoppers who added the blueprint set to their shopping cart have praised the ease of use when it came time to building their own homes.

One happy customer wrote: 

“I love these plans. Exceeded my expectations. I have been in the trades for many years and I see many plans on Etsy that are aesthetically pleasing but not practical for living or building but these plans are structurally sound, easy to incorporate and very builder friendly. This is perfect for what I was looking for to build on our ranch.”

No matter what type of foundation you opt for, the building plans have you covered.

“Looking good,” another shopper said. “Very happy at this point. Love the fact that the plans have two sets of foundation plans (one on a concrete slab and the other on concrete pillars. I’ll go the slab route!! Thank you!”

TINY HOME COMMUNITIES

While many prefer to buy the blueprints and build their tiny home themselves, you can also choose to join a prebuilt community of tiny home residents.

For instance, Sea-Vu West, a village of tiny homes in Wells, Maine, also offers spaces for rent starting at $64 a night.

The Maine community also offers the potential for long-term living in the community.

The village lists one-bedroom, one-bath options from $83,000 to $105,000.

Canoe Bay Escape Village, meanwhile, is a secluded tiny home vacation community in northwest Wisconsin.

There are eight different sizes of tiny homes available, ranging from a 263 square feet trailer for $65,000  to a 344 square feet model for $98, 923.

For residents of Northern Wisconsin and in the Minneapolis–St. Paul area, a local lender has multiple programs with as little as zero down for qualifying buyers.

But cash buyers can receive a discount averaging more than $2,000.
