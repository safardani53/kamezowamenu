Coaching with Dan Birmingham

Dan Birmingham began to coach Thurman after 2007. Under his coaching, Thurman has won many titles, including the WBA (Super) Welterweight.

Thurman and his coach Dan have a strong bonding together. They train rigorously for Boxing matches. However, he also respects his former coach Kensky Rodney and finds him a source of motivation.

Keith Thurman | Defeat with Pacquiao

Organizers finally announced Thurman’s match with Filipino Boxer Manny Pacquiao in May 2019. It happened for the WBA (Super) Welterweight world title.

At that time, Thurman was the world champion, and he wanted to retain that title again by defeating Pacquiao.

Though he boxed to win the title, he could not control himself — Pacquiao, 41, was disappointed with a final blow in the 10th round.

As a result, Thurman got defeated in the match. It was his first-ever loss in the entire Boxing career.

Keith Thurman was Depressed

After his defeat with fellow Boxer Pacquiao, Thurman was in a state of depression. The main reason for the depression was his first defeat in his career. Before the 2019 defeat, he wouldn’t have to think about this.

After 2019, he stopped playing and went for a break. He tried to control himself. He started stress-eating. As a result, Thurman weighed 87 kilos and gained extra 20 kilos in months.

But after a few months, with the help of his family and his wife, he started to feel happy. Thurman did the hard exercise for months and lost his extra 20 kilos.

Though he didn’t consult any psychiatrist, he regularly talked about the problems with his wife. Moreover, he is not depressed now.
