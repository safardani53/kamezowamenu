Emily Maynard Marries Fiance Tyler Johnson

Emily Maynard sure knows how to pull off some shock and awe. Emliy married her longtime beau Tyler in a surprise ceremony on June 7. What was believed to be just an engagement party transitioned into Emily and Tyler’s “I do’s,” E! News reveals. The invitations for the engagement party/nuptials were in a viewfinder that contained slides of pictures.

One slide featured the message, “You’re invited to Emily and Tyler’s engagement party,” according to E! News. So adorable!

The couple wed on Tyler’s family farm and only let a select number of people in on their little secret.

Emily & Tyler’s Journey To Wedding

Tyler and Emily met before she started filming The Bachelorette in 2012. Emily became engaged to Jef Holm on the show, but split six months later.

The couple rekindled their relationship after her stint on the reality show and he popped the question on January 4. He asked Emily to marry him with five breathtaking diamond bands, Us Weekly reported.
