Kylie Jenner Graduates High School!!!

If a headline mentions Kylie Jenner, it is safe to assume that she is up to no good.  For instance, recently, Kylie appeared to be driving drunk in a Snapchat video.

But, in a rare moment, Kylie Jenner has made headlines for something positive.  The reality star announced on Twitter today that she is taking it easy this summer after recently graduating high school!

The youngest daughter to Kris and Caitlyn Jenner tweeted the news in a string of weird Tweets before heading to bed early Tuesday morning.

She tweeted, "Oh AND.. I finished school last week.. & I know I never post on social media when I’m in class but that doesn’t mean…I don’t encourage education! Because I do!"

Ummmm, you shouldn’t be posting on social media when you are in class.  I’m concerned about the quality of Kylie’s education.

A source confirmed that Kylie has, in fact, received her high school diploma.

In the midst of all of the navel gazing, Kylie hasn’t said much about her education.  But, in a recent interview, big sister Kim Kardashian had positive things to say about her little sister and her schooling:

“She’s always with her friends and having fun and people would question, ‘Are you going to school?’” Kardashian told Access Hollywood in May. "She goes to school. She’s homeschooled.”

Kim added, “She goes to school every single day. Perception can be reality and people assume maybe she’s not in school because she’s not posting about being in school all the time.”

Kylie accelerated her high school program to graduate earlier.  In May, Kim said, "She has a year left.”  However, that year turned into just a few months.

Rumors have circulated that Kylie had dropped out of school.  But Kylie slammed down those rumors via Snapchat: "I do school every weekday. Again don’t believe everything you read.”

Kim, once again, defended her little sister, saying that Kylie and Kendall were intelligent.

"They know that all their friends aren’t going on private jets," Kim disclosed. "They know that they are growing up with a life of privilege, but they are super grounded and responsible.”

Most of the world would beg to differ with the sentiment that they are “grounded.”  One cannot be a narcissist and grounded.  But whatever.  I’m not here to ruin the graduation party.

With the help of being born into a famous family, Kylie is a step ahead of most recent graduates.

In February, Kylie bought her own home in Calabasas, California, for $2.7 million.  Something tells me she didn’t buy that with babysitting money or her after-school job.

"I’m moving out when I turn 18," she told Teen Vogue in May. ”I’m ready to live on my own. It’s a really big step, but I’m ready.”

Kylie continued, “I wanted a house near my family in a quiet neighborhood with a front yard and a backyard that my dog will like. Also, I have so many shoes, and none of them fit in my old closet."

You take care of those shoes, Kylie, while the rest of the graduates prepare for college loan debt.  Did Kim really say Kylie is grounded???
