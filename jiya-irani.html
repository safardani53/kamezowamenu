Jiya Irani Biography

Jiya Irani was born on July 26, 1989, in Fort Kochi, Kochi, Kerala, India. His birth date is associated with the zodiac sign Leo.

He finished his education at a local Kochi school. He has a strong interest in modeling, so he pursued it as part of his higher education. Along the way, he also learned photography. He first participated in a fashion show when he was in high school.

Jiya was raised by his parents with a lot of love and care. He has not disclosed any information about his father or mother. We also don’t know anything about his brothers and sisters. Career

Jiya Irani began his career as a model and has participated in numerous fashion shows. His perfect physique drew the attention of some directors, who gave him the opportunity to do some commercials. He promoted a variety of branded products, including luxury items. Following that, he enters the Malayalam film industry.

According to sources, he first appeared in the 2012 film ‘Mullassery Madhavan Kutty Nemom P. O.,’ directed by Kumar Nanda. In this film, he played a minor supporting role. He had the opportunity to collaborate with K.P.A.C. Lalitha, Innocent, and Sonal Devaraj.

Following the success of this film, he received an offer for the following year’s film. The film’s title was Asuravithu, and it was directed by A.K. Sajjan. It was an action film. Baburaj, Rony David, and Asif Ali played the lead roles. The film was unable to reach the top of the box office.

In addition, he directed Anandhabhadrasanam, the second installment of the Anandabhadram film. Santosh Sivan directed the film. The first installment was a box-office success, but the sequel did not fare well.
