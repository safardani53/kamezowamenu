Fiendish festive emoji quiz asks if YOU can name the Christmas movies and the record so far is 96

BANANA bread, toilet roll shortages and emoji quizzes are just a few cultural moments that came to define our first national lockdown in March.

And while we (fortunately) haven't had to put up with much stock-piling or baking social media content this time round, the emoji quiz is BACK - and this time, it's got a festive feel.

GalaBingo.com put together the fiendishly quiz - which hints at the plots of seven popular Christmas movies with up to six emojis.

And we're warning you now: it's much harder than it sounds.

According to the creators, the average player completes the tricky challenge in one minute 36 seconds.

What's more, 20 per cent of people will give up completely by the fifth puzzle... so how well will YOU do?

Answers:

  • The Holiday
  • Home Alone
  • Cool Runnings
  • Polar Express
  • It's a Wonderful Life
  • Jingle All the Way
  • Die Hard
