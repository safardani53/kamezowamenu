Photos of Iconic 90s TV Star Hollywood Life

Everyone loves Fran Drescher! The 90’s TV icon became a household name after her CBS sitcom, ‘The Nanny, delighted audiences for six seasons and you can see her best photos here.

Following the quirky shennagins of a lovable but goofy working stiff from Flushing, Queens who lands the posh job of taking care of 3 kids in Manhattan, the show’s instant success meant great things for Fran from there on out. 

From multiple follow-up series, to roles on Broadway, to two New York Times best-selling memoirs, Fran Drescher has continued to be one of the brightest stars in the biz. Aside from her work in entertainment, she is also a fierce advocate in politics, the LGBTQ community, and most notably, for furthering advancements to help women fight cancer. In fact, after suriving her own cancer battle, she started the Cancer Schmancer foundation that has been doing incredible work for over a decade.
