Are You Going? Hollywood Life

Edward and Bella will officially tie the knot in an evening ceremony on August 13 — that’s just 68 days away! Check out their gorgeous and delicate wedding invitations!

The wedding of the year is right around the corner! MTV has just given us the first glimpse at Robert Pattinson and Kristen Stewart‘s Breaking Dawn wedding invitation. The simple card invites friends and family of the couple to their highly anticipated outdoor ceremony, which will take place at 5 o’clock at 420 Woodcroft Ave, Forks, WA — so if you’re in the area you should definitely stop by!

“Isabella Maria Swan and Edward Anthony Masen Cullen together with their families request the honor of your presence at the celebration of their marriage, Saturday the thirteenth of August two thousand and eleven five o’clock in the evening.”

We know from the exclusive sneak peek clip shown during the MTV Movie Awards that Jacob [Taylor Lautner] isn’t very happy when he receives his invitation. Do you think he’ll do something to ruin their big day?

But nothing could take away from the fact that Kristen’s hair and dress look absolutely stunning in the clip — it took our breath away!

The moment we’ve all been waiting for is in just a few months and even though it will be a simple yet elegant ceremony the buzz around it will certainly top Kim Kardashian‘s summer nuptials and maybe even the royal wedding!
