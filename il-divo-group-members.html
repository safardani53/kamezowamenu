Il Divo: Who are the members of the group?

SINCE 2003, Il Divo has dominated the music charts in the United Kingdom after being created by music producer Simon Cowell.

In December 2021, the multi-national classical crossover vocal group mourned the loss of one of their own after it was revealed Carlos Marín passed away at the age of 53.

Il Divo: Who are the members of the group?

Il Divo is made up of four tenors from France, Switzerland, Spain, and the United States.

The music group's members include:

Urs Bühler

Born July 19, 1971, Urs Bühler, 50, is famously known as a Swiss classically trained tenor.

Since 2016, he has been married to Leticia Martin Crespo and the two have since welcomed a baby girl, Billie.

Carlos Marin

Born October 13, 1968, in Rüsselsheim, Germany, Carlos Marin, was famously known as a Spanish baritone.

On December 19, 2021, it was announced that he passed away after he was placed in a coma due to a Covid-19 diagnosis.

Marin had been in the music industry his entire life and released his first album when he was just eight-years-old.

Over the years Marin also starred in musicals including Les Misérables and The Beauty and the Beast.

David Miller

Born April 14, 1973, David Miller, 48, is famously known as an American tenor.

The San Diego native is the son of Donal Leigh Miller, who is famously known as a biographer and historian.

Since 2009, he has been married to Sarah Joy Kabanuck, 35.

Sébastien Izambard

Born March 7, 1973, Sébastien Izambard, 48, is famously known as a French musician, singer, composer, and record producer.

How many albums has Il Divo sold?

Since the group formed in 2003, they have released 11 albums, including a Motown tribute album which features the band's cover of the hit "Ain't No Mountain High Enough," alongside the late Marvin Gaye.

Over the years, they have been able to sell over 30million albums worldwide.
