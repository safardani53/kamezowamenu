I'm fed up of fruit flies in my kitchen - here's how to get rid of them for free using ingredients i

WHILE many of us are thrilled to finally see some sunshine, with the warmer weather comes some rather unwanted guests.

Fruit flies are out in full force and are either swarming around the fruit bowl or whizzing around your head.

So, if you're looking for a quick and easy solution to wave goodbye to the pesky flies for good, then you're in luck...

A cleaning whizz named Charlene has shared her top tip on getting rid of them for free - using ingredients you'll already have in your kitchen cupboards.

In a clip shared to TikTok (@mumscleaninglockdownlife), she asks: "Anyone else fed up of fruit flies?"

Sharing her simple solution, Charlene then fills up a small plastic tub with Elbow Grease washing up liquid, followed by a dash of vinegar.

The cleaning guru then places a layer of cling film over the tub and pushes her finger through to create a hole big enough for the fruit flies to get inside.

Popping it on the top of her bin, she then waits for the miracle mixture to work its magic.
