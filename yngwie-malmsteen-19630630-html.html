Yngwie Johan Malmsteen is a Swedish guitarist, musician, composer and band. Malmsteen was first known in the 1980s for his neoclassical style of playing in the heavy metal genre, and has released 20 studio albums over a career spanning more than 40 years. In 2009, Time magazine ranked Malmsteen ninth among the 11 greatest electric guitarists of all time.
