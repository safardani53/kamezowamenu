Matt Ryan’s Net Worth & Salary

Undoubtedly, the American football star has accumulated wealth through his professional career and endorsement deals. 

In 2008, Ryan signed a six-year contract of $72 million with the Atlanta Falcons. In addition, the agreement gave $34.75 million as guaranteed money.

Later in 2014, Matt signed a 5-year contract extension. Its estimated net worth is $103.75 million, including $12 million as the optional bonus and a $28 million signing bonus. 

Likewise, Ryan made it into the 2020 Forbes Highest-Paid Athletes list. Similarly, he was also ranked the 47th highest-paid with $29.2 million as his earnings.

He was the first NFL player to earn $30 million from his salary. In the 2020 season, Matt Ryan received $20,500,000 as his salary.

After the deal’s expiration, Matt again signed a $103.75 million five-year extension contract in July 2013. 

Later, in May 2018, Matt Ryan signed a five-year $180 million contract with Atlanta Falcons, making him the league’s highest-paid star.

Matt Ryan: Career & Achievements

Matthew Thomas Ryan was born on May 17, 1985, in Exton, Pennsylvania, USA, to Michael Ryan and Bernie Ryan.

Likewise, Ryan started his football journey while playing for the Boston Eagles of Boston College.

He won the 2004 Freshman Male Scholar-Athlete during his first season. Later, he was the ACC Player of the Year for 2007 and was chosen for the First-team All-American.

Over six years, he signed a contract worth $72 million with over $34 million guaranteed, which helped to increase his net worth.

He was also chosen for the Pro Bowl game in 2010, 2012, and 2014. Later in 2013, Matt was offered a contract extension worth $103.75 million for five years, which increased his net worth.

Moreover, Ryan is the Atlanta Falcon’s career Leader in passing completions, passing yards, passing touchdowns, passing attempts, and winning.

To date, he has only missed two games in eight seasons.
