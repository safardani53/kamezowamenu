NICK Cannon took his kids with Mariah Carey, Moroccan and Monroe, to a "cat cafe" as he remains silent on Abby De La Rosa giving birth to their twins this month.

The Wild ’N Out host treated his 10-year-olds to the fun day and revealed that it was the family’s first time EVER petting a cat.

On Sunday, Nick, 40, went to Java Cats in Georgia which is a coffee lounge that offers customers the chance to adopted kitties in need of a home.

Patrons can also pet and play with the animals - or even bottle feed some of the fosters.

The TV star shared a series of glimpses into their adventures, with adorable snaps of his daughter hugging the cats.

Proud dad Nick said that it was her idea to bring her family to the establishment, sharing a picture of Monroe and writing: “One of the most loving and beautiful spirits God has ever placed on this planet had a great idea to visit foster and adopted cats.

“I would’ve never thought of something so amazing. She convinced me and her brother Roc and we all had an amazing time.”

The owner of the Atlanta-based cafe shared some memories of her own, and said that growing up as a “huge 90’s Nickelodeon” fan made the day a “cool experience.”

She said of Nick and his kids: “Neither had EVER pet a cat before, so what an honor for Java Cats to be the very first place for their interactions with cats!

“I was able to show Monroe and Moroccan how to approach, pet, and play with our lounge kitties and even bottle feed some of our fosters... They couldn’t have been a sweeter family. Thank you all for supporting Java Cats!!!”

While the entertainer certainly looked like he had quite the day with his oldest kids, this comes as he's yet to comment on his latest children with Abby.

Earlier this month, the DJ welcomed welcomed twin boys, Zion and Zillion.

The proud mom shared the happy news on social media with an intimate post that showed her cuddling with her sweet baby boys, however, Nick was nowhere to be found in any of the posts shared.

On Father's Day, though, Abby confirmed The Masked Singer host was in attendance at the hospital, as she shared a photo of him holding their little ones with the caption, "grateful."

Despite the confirmation, Nick has yet to comment on his new baby boys, however, he recently posed with his new baby mama, Alyssa Scott.

In the photos, Nick could be seen cuddling up to Alyssa as he cradled her big belly.
