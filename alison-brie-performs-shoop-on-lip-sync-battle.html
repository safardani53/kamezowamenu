Alison Brie Performs &quot;Shoop&quot; on Lip Sync Battle

Alison Brie is yet to actually perform on Lip Sync Battle.

But we’re prepared to add her routine to our Best of the Bunch video gallery.

Following a brief hiatus, this Spike TV competition will return on July 9 with the Community and Mad Men star putting on quite a show to “Shoop,” the 1993 smash hit by Salt-N-Pepa.

In this sneak peek, co-hosts Chrissy Teigen and LL Cool J rock out right alongside the 32-year-old beauty, while opponent Will Arnett who looks absolutely terrified to try and top her sexy performance.

Lip Sync Battle airs at 10/9c on Spike.
