A BRAND new snack spread has just launched from two beloved brands and fans are loving the offering.

Challenge Butter and Lawry's have teamed up to create a new butter-based, seasoned snack spread - Seasoned Salt.

The new variety is a mix of Challenge Butter and the iconic Lawry's signature blend of salt, herbs, and spices. 

Customers can spread, top, and drizzle the Seasoned Salt on all kinds of their favorite foods like baked potatoes, french fries, grilled corn, and chicken.

The companies describe the spread as having a “unique flavor and added zing” to each bite it’s placed on.

"Our Butter Snack Spread line is designed to bring more flavor to our consumers so we are thrilled to add Lawry's, a family favorite kitchen staple to our latest offering," Michael Burdeny, president of Challenge Dairy Products, Inc. said in a press release.

"Challenge and Lawry's products have been loved for generations and we predict fans of both will be ecstatic to easily elevate their snacks and meals with this new versatile butter," he added.

Customers have taken to Facebook to rave about the product.

Most Challenge Butter Snack Spreads are available in 6.5-ounce tubs at a suggested retail price of $3.99.

Customers can grab them only in select retailers nationwide including Walmart, Albertsons, Meijer, HEB, and more.

SWEET SPREAD

In addition to the salty spread, the company has also added a new dessert flavor, Lemon.

The addition is said to balance tartness and sweetness all blended with "fresh buttery goodness."

Challenge Butter suggests spreading it on blueberry muffins, bagels, pancakes, croissants, and more.

These new flavors have come alongside its other offerings like Chocolate, Vanilla Fudge, Salted Caramel, and Everything and Garlic Parmesan with Herbs.

MORE NEW FOODS

Another company to add to its collection of savory options is Takis.

The iconic chip maker launched its newest creation - Dragon Sweet Chili.

Each chip comes with sweet and spicy flavors, all rolled into a sweet chili-flavored tortilla chip.

Fans can expect a spicy, sweet, chili, pepper, and lime flavor with its famous Taki shape.

Takis said its newest fire-breathing addition offers the “classic Takis intensity with a touch of electrifying sweet heat.”

Keebler has also added a new product to celebrate National Fudge Day this year.

The Chips Deluxe Fudgy is its newest cookie creation and is said to be “fudgier than ever,” as it's made with even more chocolate chips and fudge chunks for the sweet eaters.

The new treats will roll out across retailers nationwide for a suggested retail price of $4.80, per a company press release.
