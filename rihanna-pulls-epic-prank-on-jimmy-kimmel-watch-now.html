Rihanna Pulls Epic Prank on Jimmy Kimmel: Watch Now!

So… how does it feel, Jimmy Kimmel?!?

The talk show host – whose memorable pranks have included this epic Twerking fail and this crazy Christmas decorating of John Krasinski’s house – ended up the victim of a different celebrity’s prank on Wednesday morning.

Just one hour into April Fools’ Day, Rihanna invaded Kimmel’s home.

Let in by the comedian’s wife and armed with music, money and confetti cannon, the singer starting blasting her new single, “Bitch Better Have My Money,” waking up Kimmel (and his mouthguard!) in a foggy haze at 1 a.m.

“What is happening?” a groggy Kimmel asks in the footage featured above. “What the f-ck is going on?"

This is what you get, Jimmy, when you have parents tell their kids they ate their Halloween candy.

Rihanna was then a guest on Jimmy Kimmel Live last night, prompting the host to get in the final say/joke on the matter:

"I will admit,” he told the sexy artist, “that’s not usually how you come to me in my dreams.”

Well done, Rihanna. And well handled, Jimmy Kimmel. Nice piece of dental equipment!
