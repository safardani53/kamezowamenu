NBA YoungBoy &amp; Jazlyn Mychelle Share Flirty Video As YaYa Shows Bump Hollywood Life

Cue the latest plot twist of NBA YoungBoy‘s love life: the “Kacey talk” rapper reunited with Jazlyn Mychelle, who he was romantically linked to in the summer of 2020, to film a flirty video on Triller. NBA YoungBoy, 21, happily cuddled and danced with the YouTuber throughout the video, and Jazyln’s dog even joined the dance party. The affectionate video left some fans confused, who thought NBA YoungBoy was dating Floyd Mayweather‘s daughter, Iyanna “YaYa” Mayweather, 20, whom he is currently expecting a child with.

“So he is not with yaya I really am always behind,” one person commented underneath Jazlyn’s video, and another fan responded, “it’s not you alone girl.” Not even a full two weeks ago, YaYa revealed that she was “taken” in a TikTok video, and fans took this to mean that she was fully back together with the father of her child.

A few hours before Jazlyn posted her PDA with NBA YoungBoy, YaYa showed off her baby bump in a solo mirror selfie while staying cozy in a Christmas-themed onesie. “It’s beginning to look a lot like Christmas,” read a GIF that YaYa added to the festive maternity photo.

NBA YoungBoy has had an on-again, off-again relationship with YaYa since they were first linked together at the end of Dec. 2018. By the summer of 2020, fans wondered if NBA YoungBoy started dating someone new after he was seen in an Instagram Story Boomerang with another woman, which The Shade Room reposted in June. Some fans identified the woman as Jazlyn in the comments section, although HollywoodLife can not independently confirm these claims. Later, a video of the rapper hanging out by the pool with a woman named “Jaz” was uploaded by the Durtty Daily YouTube channel in July (which you can watch below).

NBA YoungBoy recently welcomed his sixth child, a daughter named Kodi Capri, on Nov. 26, according to The Shade Room. One of the mothers of his children, Drea Symone, revealed the happy news in an Instagram post on Dec. 1. This means NBA YoungBoy will be a father of seven once his baby with YaYa arrives! YaYa’s famous father, Floyd, confirmed the pregnancy on the Oct. 31 episode of Hollywood Unlocked.
