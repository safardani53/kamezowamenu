Eileen Davidson Hollywood Life

Bio:
Elieen Davidson (born Eileen Davidson in Artesia, California on June 15, 1959) is an American reality personality, actress and former model. She is best known for her roles on soap operas Days Of Our Lives, The Bold and the Beautiful and The Young and the Restless. She joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills for Season 5.

Best Known For:
Eileen is best known for starring as Kristen DiMera on Days of Our Lives and playing the same character Ashley Abbott on both The Bold and the Beautiful and The Young and the Restless, which she joined in 1984. She won the Daytime Emmy award for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama Series in 2014 for her work on Days Of Our Lives.

Personal Life:
Eileen has been married three times. She is currently married to actor Vincent Van Patten (married in 2003), and they share one child together, Jesse, born in 2003. Eileen was previously married to actor Christopher Mayer (1985-86) and soap opera star Jon Lindstrom (1997-2000).
