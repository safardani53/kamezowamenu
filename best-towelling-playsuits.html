Best Towelling Playsuits 2021 | The Sun UK

TOWELLING playsuits are your next must-have for summer - and the preppy trend is available in all sorts of pastel colours.

If the last time you wore towelling was when you sipped your morning cuppa, then you need to know about the best towelling playsuits.

Yes, your dressing gown might just be on-trend now. Ok, maybe not. Towelling playsuits are chic, effortless and such an easy outfit go-to.

Terry cloth is no stranger to fashion though: Cindy Crawford's Chanel 1996 runway bikini-cardi combo will forever be one of our favourites.

Today, Hailey Bieber, Kylie Jenner and just about every influencer on your feed is jumping on the terry-towelling wave.

There's never been such a perfect time to review the best towelling playsuits to shop in the UK. So, that's what we did...

    Where to buy towelling playsuits?

    Now that terry-towelling is having a moment, so many high-street stores have their own takes on the trend.

    ASOS, Urban Outfitters, Mango, COS and H&M are all among the many retailers jumping on the wave with towelling playsuits.

    Keep your eye out because we don't think the trend is going anywhere.

    What to look for in a towelling playsuit?

    Comfort is key. You want a snug number that'll flatter your figure in a way that makes you feel confident.

    Some people like bodycon playsuits whereas others opt for looser styles that come with a belt to cinch your waist.

    When looking for a playsuit, simply find one that makes you feel and look good.

    Are towelling playsuits in style in 2021?

    Whether it's due to Hailey Bieber's terry cloth bikinis or how well they look with this season's favourite pastel tones, towelling playsuits are in.

    Bottega Veneta, The Row, Zimmermann and Dries Van Noten are all in on the terry cloth comeback.

    And they've definitely got us wishing for warm weather all year round.
