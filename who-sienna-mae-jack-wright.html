What is the 'what Sienna Mae did to me' video about?

SIENNA Mae Gomez is a TikTok star and part of the Hype House collective.

Here is what we know about her and the recent allegations that have surfaced. 

Who is Sienna Mae?

Sienna Mae Gomez is known as a body-positive TikToker who is a member of the content collective the Hype House.

She has more than 21million followers on her two TikTok accounts.

The 17-year-old star, who is also a competitive dancer, has grown to become one of the platform's biggest influencers.

This came after one of her videos went viral in August 2020, catapulting her to online stardom.

Since then she has signed up with a talent agency as well as partnering with cosmetics brand Maybelline.

She is now a starring member of Netflix's upcoming Hype House reality series following the TikTok content collective she is part of.

More on bloggers and influencers

What is the 'what Sienna Mae did to me' video about?

Following an abrupt stop to Jack Wright and Gomez' TikTok collaborations in late 2020, content creator Mason Rizzo tweeted allegations that Gomez had "sexually assaulted" and "abused" Wright in May 2021. 

Gomez responded to the charges many times, adamantly denying she assaulted Wright.

Until now, Wright has remained relatively silent on the topic, claiming that the two sides were working things out "offline." 

However, on January 11, 2022, he posted a TikTok indicating that he couldn't "stay quiet anymore," and on January 20, he broke down while narrating his experience in a 17-minute YouTube video titled What Sienna Mae Did to Me.

In the video, Wright starts off by saying: "I've sat down many times trying to do this. It's been very hard."

Then, he broke down his claims against Gomez, detailing everything from her relationship with his friend to her alleged assaults.

Gomez has yet to respond to these allegations.

Who else has Sienna Mae dated?

In 2020, she dated someone known on Instagram as @charbankss for more than half of the year.

The pair later broke up for an unknown reason and never addressed it with fans.

Gomez then began to reportedly date Wright in 2021, and fans began to suspect that the two had broken up by May of the same year.
