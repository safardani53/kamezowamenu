Who is Karamoko Dembele:

Karamoko Dembele is a famous Soccer Player. He was born on February 22, 2003 and his birthplace is London, England. Karamoko is also well known as, Right wing forward who rose to fame for coming up in the Celtic FC system. In October 2016, he garnered major media attention for debuting as a 13 year old with Celtic FC’s U-20 squad, and signed a youth contract to remain with Celtic in 2017. 

Karamoko is originated from England. He was invited to practice with Celtic FC’s first team squad at age 13 by the team’s then-new head coach Brendan Rodgers.
