Tyler Reddick Parents Clarence And Geri Are His Inspiration

Tyler Reddick was born to Geri and Clarence Reddick. His parents have been highly supportive of his career. Their unwavering motivation and inspiration have positioned Tyler for great success.

Tyler George Reddick, a.k.a. Tyler Reddick, born on January 11, 1996, is a professional stock car racing driver from the United States.

Currently, he is a full-time competitor in the NASCAR Cup Series. He drives the No. 45 Toyota Camry for 23XI Racing.

Additionally, Reddick participates part-time in the NASCAR Xfinity Series, driving the No. 24 Toyota Supra for Sam Hunt Racing.

He has achieved remarkable success in the Xfinity Series, securing consecutive championships in 2018 and 2019.

Reddick holds the record for the closest finish in NASCAR’s top three series, narrowly defeating Elliott Sadler at Daytona International Speedway during the 2018 Xfinity Series season.

On October 15, 2022, 23XI Racing announced that they had acquired Tyler Reddick’s contract from RCR, thus enabling him to replace Kurt Busch and drive the No. 45 car for the 2023 season.

Following the development, Reddick commenced the 2023 season with a disappointing 39th-place did-not-finish (DNF) at the Daytona 500.

However, he swiftly redeemed himself a month later by securing his first season victory in a thrilling triple-overtime race at COTA.

Tyler Reddick Parents

Tyler was born in Corning, California, to Gerri and Clarence Reddick. From the beginning, his parents recognized and nurtured his passion for racing, becoming his unwavering pillar of encouragement and motivation.

Brimming with paternal pride, Clarence states himself as “a very proud dad” on his LinkedIn profile, showcasing his support for Tyler’s endeavors.

Not stopping there, Gerri and Clarence have taken their parental dedication to the digital realm.

They have created a joint Instagram handle dedicated to sharing the remarkable journey of Tyler, highlighting his thrilling tours and accomplishments. 

Geri and Clarence have been Tyler’s biggest cheerleaders. They have attended his races, offering encouragement during triumphs and setbacks. Their belief in his abilities has been unwavering.

Furthermore, Clarence’s guidance to Tyler for more than 15 years has profoundly impacted Tyler’s approach to racing. 

More On Father And Mother

Tyler’s father, Clarence, has an extensive 25-year background in the automotive industry, starting from entry-level positions and advancing to upper management and partnerships.

After transitioning to IT work, he dedicated 15 years to supporting Tyler’s development as a professional race car driver. He is also a pilot of the new innovative SF50 Vision Jet made by Cirrus Aircraft.

Currently, Clarence focuses on forging beneficial partnerships, including associations with JR Motorsports, BurgerFi, Nationwide Children’s Hospital, Amour Chili, Pinnacle Financial, and Takl.

In his LinkedIn profile, he states that the Reddick family has experienced success in various ventures, such as founding Broken Bow Music Group and owning five auto dealerships in California.

They significantly impacted the racing industry, winning the closest race in NASCAR history.

Geri Reddick, Tyler’s mother, a central figure in Tyler’s career, remains relatively private. Limited information is available about her.

While her name is recognized and her significant role in shaping Tyler’s racing journey is acknowledged, details about her background and contributions beyond her involvement in his career remain undisclosed.
