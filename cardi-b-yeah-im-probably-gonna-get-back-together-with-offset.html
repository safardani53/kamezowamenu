Yeah, I'm Probably Gonna Get Back Together With Offset

It’s only been a few days since the world learned that Cardi B and Offset have called it quits after about 18 seconds of marriage.

Cardi B was the one to pull the plug, and she had a number of good reasons for doing so.

Basically, Offset took cheating to a new level, engaging in multiple affairs which allegedly involved threesomes and multiple sex tapes.

For obvious reasons, Cardi’s fans were happy to her kick her cheating baby daddy to the curb.

And now, many are chagrinned by the news that Offset hopes to save his marriage.

Astonishingly, it seems like he may get his wish, as a source confirms to E! News that the Migos rapper is "trying to win Cardi back" and adds that she’s "not on board yet." 

That "yet" has fans concerned, as reports indicate that Cardi still imagines a future with Offset.

"They haven’t been spending time together and Cardi expressed she still wants her space," the insider says.

"She is very busy right now with her work and is focused solely on Kulture." 

The source confirms that Cardi "still loves" Offset "and wants her family to be whole again."

"She has her up and down moments where she thinks there could be a chance they can salvage their relationship," one insider claims.

"But then Cardi is also very upset over the cheating situation. She is very torn and is trying to stay focused on business matters right now.

"She doesn’t want to have to co-parent Kulture, but isn’t quite sure if they can make their relationship work.

Cardi reportedly has yet to begin divorce proceedings, due to the fact that  "there is still a chance of reconciliation."

In other Cardi news, she recently went off on a fan who suggested the divorce drama was merely a publicity stunt:

"I wouldn’t put my family in a bad name for no f–king publicity, ’cause at the end of the day, 10 years from now, my daughter, she’s gonna be looking at these type of things and she’s gonna be asking me about these type of things," Cardi said in an Instagram Story.

"My relationship was never fake. I met this n—a, he was on my ass, I gave him a chance and we fell in love with each other. Things was not always gravy, ’cause at the end of the day, we are two different people.

"But we never did anything for f–king publicity, bitch. You think I got f–king pregnant, could have ruined my career for f–king publicity?"

As for Offset, his efforts to win Cardi back have been complicated by the fact that new mistresses keep emerging.

According to OK! magazine, an Australian Instagram model named Sophia Alia is currently running damage control after a sex tape featuring her and Offset leaked online.

“It was a private moment that she did not consent to being filmed,” a source tells the tabloid.

“She is horrified it’s gone viral and she’s been dragged into Cardi’s split.”

Here’s hoping Cardi gets those divorce papers in ASAP.
