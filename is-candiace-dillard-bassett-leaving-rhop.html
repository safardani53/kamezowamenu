Candiace Dillard Bassett Always Had a Plan for Her RHOP Exit Is She Leaving?

Candiace Dillard Bassett has hinted her time on 'Real Housewives of Potomac' could end as her career goals expand. Here's the tea.

The Gist:

  • Candiace Dillard Bassett has starred on The Real Housewives of Potomac since Season 3.
  • Candiace says she has an "exit strategy" for leaving the series, but doesn't have a end date yet.
  • Candiace says her feuds with Gizelle Bryant and Robyn Dixon could end with an "apology" from them.

From the time she arrived at The Real Housewives of Potomac during the Bravo show’s second season, Candiace Dillard Bassett has successfully kept fans coming back for more. Her succinct reads and impeccable vocabulary can bring even the toughest ‘Wife to tears. And when it’s time for Candiace to shed a tear, you know she will do so with her iconic “cry-angle” napkin fold.

As endearing as it’s been to watch Candiace on our screens for years, every Housewives star faces the moment they must hang up their wine glass and remove the cameras from their personal lives. Since RHOP returned for its eighth season in November 2023, Candiace has hinted she could become a former reality TV star sooner rather than later.

Here’s everything Candiace has said about how long she plans on staying on RHOP.

Is Candiace Dillard Bassett leaving ‘The Real Housewives of Potomac?’

RHOP returned to Bravo for Season 8 on Nov. 5, 2023. Many fans noticed this season that Candiace, known to get into an altercation or two, kept her distance around her friend group during the season. Candiace has even said she considers Wendy Osefo her only authentic friend in the cast.

With Candiace staying out of the drama in Season 8, fans became concerned the season would be her last. In December 2023, Candiace admitted to Entertainment Tonight that she was stepping back from getting into the drama but wasn’t planning on leaving Potomac for good.

However, the “Drive Back” singer said she has an “exit strategy” for reality TV in her purview. She has already been focused on her ventures outside of Bravo, including her music and a starring role on the AllBLK Network’s Hush.

"I have always had a plan," Candiace explained about her future on RHOP. "Everything is a gift. All of this, like the music, the acting, Potomac -- it's all a bonus for me. None of it is something that I feel entitled to, and I would rather live that way than the other way."

In January 2023, Candiace made similar sentiments when Page Six asked her about her time on RHOP, making it clear that she would know when it was time.

“I’m here until I’m not or until they fire me,” she explained.

Candiace Dillard Bassett has a history of not getting along with her ‘RHOP’ co-stars.

Although she’s not an OG, Candiace has become synonymous with RHOP during her time on the show. She has been at the center of several of the series’ scandals, including her epic fight with her former co-star, Monique Samuels, and her Season 7 beef with Gizelle Bryant that has continued into Season 8.

In Season 7, Gizelle claimed Candiace’s husband, Chris Bassett, made Gizelle feel uncomfortable during the taping of the RHOP Season 6 reunion. The accusation caused Candiace and Gizelle’s friendship to dissolve and, consequently, Candiace’s friendship with Gizelle’s BFF, Robyn Dixon. Candiace and Robyn also came to blows after Candiace tweeted her disappointment in Robyn for not disclosing her marital issues with her husband, Juan Dixon, on the show.

“If you would just shut the f–-k up and worry about your boyfriends for hire and cheating husbands, you wouldn’t have to post ‘proof’ that you weren’t behind yet another plot against someone’s family,” Candiace said in October 2023, per Us Weekly. “Once again, the people of God are tired.”

In her interview with ET, Candiace said she would be willing to turn over a new leaf with Gizelle and Robyn, significantly shifting the current dynamics within the cast. However, Candiace said she wouldn’t do so without an apology from her co-stars and former pals.

“I mean, I'm probably crazy — I'm still optimistic that people could come to their senses," Candiace admitted. "But I'm also prepared to move around in the event that they're not, but I will also make clear, what I will not do is pretend like I'm not owed an apology and like I'm not owed humanity from people who want to be inhumane."

Fortunately, fans will have plenty of time to see what unfolds between Candiace and her castmates since we will have more of the multitalented Bravolebrity in future RHOP seasons!
