Christina Aguilera's daughter Summer, 8, looks so tall in mom's rare new photo- but fans are all ask

SUMMER Rain Rutler, the rarely-seen daughter of Christina Aguilera, has grown tall in a series of new photos.

Christina, 42, shared photos and video from her trip to New York.

Before heading over to Broadway for The Lion King musical, the mother-daughter duo made a stop at the Museum of Ice Cream.

In the Instagram collection, the Genie In A Bottle singer wore a brownish rain poncho with a tight white top and denim jeans. 

The pop singer pulled her blond hair back and covered her eyes with sunglasses. 

Summer, eight, appeared to be having fun in the ball pit and smiled while posing with a teeny tiny sundae dish.

FAMILY TIME

In February 2022, the Lady Marmalade singer gave her followers a glimpse into her family life.

In the video/photo montage, the Grammy winner gathered the kids together with her fiancé Matthew Rutler.

In the black and white slideshow, the throwback photos followed the wholesome family going on adventures, celebrating numerous holidays, and laughing and playing together.

Christina filmed herself dancing with her baby girl and posed in multiple snaps with the group.

In the family photo, Matt posed by the dock with Christina, the kids, and their family dog.

In the other shot, Matt is seen pushing his daughter on the swing set and cuddling with his baby girl in the next.

The lyrics to the Frank Ocean song, Godspeed, appeared in the sweet clip.

She captioned the post: "My greatest loves of all time…"
