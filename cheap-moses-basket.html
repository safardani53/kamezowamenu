Cheapest and Best Value Moses Baskets 2020 | The Sun UK

A MOSES basket is a portable bassinet for baby to sleep in or lie down in when you need a hands-free moment.

Moses baskets may be essential in those early months of baby’s life, but you don’t need to get a super-expensive one baby will quickly outgrow. Here's a selection of the best value Moses baskets from your favourite retailers which all cost £50 or less.

A Moses basket is one of the main options of first baby bed (along with a crib or carrycot) for newborn babies.

Typically, Moses baskets are made of natural materials like palm, banana leaf or wicker to create sturdiness and natural ventilation. Most feature an adjustable hood, padded cotton mattress and some bedding.

You can buy a separate Moses basket stand which is either stationary or moves back and forth, if you want to be able to rock your baby to sleep.

Alternatively, you can ditch the stand altogether and place Moses basket near your bed on the floor in your room – a safer choice than placing it on a raised surface that isn't designed to support it.

Moses baskets are ultra-convenient because they’re so portable. You can easily transport a Moses basket from room to room so baby can nap wherever you are in the day - just don't do it while baby is inside for safety reasons.

Typically, Moses baskets are designed for newborns and shouldn’t be used beyond three or four months of age (six in some cases, depending on whether baby is able to roll over or sit up), which is why finding a cheap and lovely-looking Moses basket will prove useful, so you don’t need to spend unnecessary funds.

Bonus: you can use it as a fab toy display in the nursery once your baby has grown too large for it.
