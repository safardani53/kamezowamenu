Look at My &quot;Kylie Jenner Lips!&quot;

In her latest Instagram selfie, Nicole "Snooki" Polizzi is doing her best impression of a member of the Kardashian family.

No, she isn’t filming herself having sex with Ray J. Nor is she screaming at Scott Disick to quit drinking.

But the former Jersey Shore cast member is making like Kylie Jenner… huge lips style!

"Trying to do Kylie Jenner lips," the mother of two captioned the selfie above, referring to the impressively full lips of the 17-year-old reality star.

Jenner, of course, has denied on multiple occasions that she’s had any plastic surgery done… but have you seen these Kylie Jenner photos?!? They make it difficult for us to take her seriously.

Last month, Snooki wrote about Jenner’s lips on her blog, admiring the look, but joining the chorus of those who believe they are the result of injections.

"I love Kylie the most out of all the Kardashians and Jenners," Polizzi wrote, "And that’s big for me. She’s so hot and I love her lips. Those are lip injections….she definitely got a little bit but a lot of it is the liner."

What has Kylie had to say about these rumors?

"You guys have watched me grow up since I was 9. My face is going to get different,” she told Cosmo late last year. “I [just] know how to do my makeup, contour and everything."
