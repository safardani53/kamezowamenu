What happened to Ryan Coogler?

RYAN Coogler is famously known as an American filmmaker who rose to fame after winning the top audience and grand jury awards at the 2013 Sundance Film Festival.

In March 2022, the star made headlines after it was revealed that he had been handcuffed outside a Bank of America location in Atlanta and fans want to know what he allegedly did.

Who is Ryan Coogler?

Born May 23, 1986, Coogler, 35, is known as a filmmaker and director in the movie industry.

Over the years he has worked on a number of films but he is arguably most famous for his work on Marvel's Black Panther and Creed.

Other productions Coogler has been a part of include Judas and the Black Messiah and Space Jam: A New Legacy.

Coogler is also working on the upcoming films, Black Panther: Wakanda Forever and Creed III, according to IMDb.

What happened to Ryan Coogler?

On March 9, 2022, it was revealed that Coogler had been placed in handcuffs while visiting a Bank of America location in Atlanta in January.

At the time, a staff member believed that he was attempting to rob the bank after trying to make a withdrawal from his account, according to TMZ.

"This situation should never have happened. However, Bank of America worked with me and addressed it to my satisfaction and we have moved on," Coogler said.

At the time of the incident, Coogler was wearing sunglasses and a facemask and had reportedly given the bank teller a note that read: "I would like to withdraw $12,000 cash from my checking account. Please do the money count somewhere else. I'd like to be discreet."

Because of the note and an alert on the teller's computer, the bank got suspicious and police were called, later detaining Coogler and two people waiting outside for him, TMZ reported.

Coogler and the people who were detained with him were later let go.

He is reportedly in Atlanta to film the Black Panther sequel which is scheduled to be released on November 11, 2022.

What is Ryan Coogler's net worth?

A career in the entertainment industry often comes with a large payday and Coogler's is no exception.

As of March 2022, he has an estimated net worth of $25million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

This number comes from his film work which has reportedly earned over $1.4billion worldwide.

Coogler has also won a number of awards over the years and was featured on Time's 2013 30 People Under 30 Changing the World list and was also the runner-up for Time's Person of the Year award in 2018.

In 2021, Coogler was also nominated for an Oscar award for his work on Judas and the Black Messiah.
