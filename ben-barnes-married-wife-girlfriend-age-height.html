Is Ben Barnes Married To A Wife Now? Into His Dating Life

Ben Barnes, the talented English actor plus singer, has captivated audiences across the globe with his versatile performances and soulful music. He is widely recognized for his outstanding performances in various hit movies and television series.

Born on August 20, 1981, in London, Barnes developed a passion for acting at an early age. He attended Homefield Preparatory and King’s College School, where he was actively involved in drama and music programs. He later enrolled at Kingston University, where he studied drama and English literature.

His breakthrough role came in 2008 when he portrayed Prince Caspian in the blockbuster film series, The Chronicles of Narnia. He then went on to star in several other successful movies and TV shows, including Stardust, Dorian Gray, Westworld, and The Punisher.

Apart from acting, Barnes is also an accomplished singer and musician. In 2021, he released his debut single, ’11:11,’ and later released his debut EP, Songs For You, featuring the songs ’11:11,’ ‘Rise Up,’ ‘Pirate Song,’ ‘Not The End,’ and ‘Ordinary Day.’

His music has received widespread praise and has further demonstrated his creative abilities and musical talents.

But in recent years, he is famously known for his role as General Kirigan/The Darkling in the Netflix series Shadow and Bone, which also stars actors such as Jessie Mei Li, Freddy Carter, Amita Suman, Kit Young, and Archie Renaux.

With his rising popularity over the years, thanks to his great performances, people are now curious about his personal life, especially his age, height, and whether he has a girlfriend or is already married. And if married, who’s his wife?

Who is Ben Barnes married to? Wife details?

Many fangirls might be glad to know that Ben Barnes is single at present. And he has not been married yet, too.

The handsome and charismatic actor was last reported to be dating American dancer Julianne Hough. The news of them dating each other came out after they were seen getting intimate on an ice cream date in January 2021.

Ben Barnes and Julianne Hough enjoying their walk and ice cream. (Photo: Dailymail)

The pair were enjoying each moment with each other as they stepped out of the ice cream parlor, walked in the streets, and shared some candid smiles.

Their casual dressing also complimented their chemistry — Ben wore black shorts, compression leggings, and a grey Nike jacket on top, whereas Julianne flaunted black leggings, a black sherpa jacket, and athletic sneakers.

Moreover, the dating news between Ben and Julianne became much stronger with the fact that Julianne announced her separation from Brooks Laich, her husband of three years, in the spring of 2020 and the divorce proceedings in November.

However, a source close to People magazine denied the possible romance between them, saying that the two have been friends for eight years and “there is nothing more to it.”

But on the possible side, both of them were single [in 2021], and it could be possible that they might have dated each other at some point. That being said, as of 2023, they have not confirmed dating one another or any other partner, for that matter.

Ben Barnes’ past relationships and girlfriends

Ben Barnes had been in a couple of relationships [and, therefore, girlfriends] in the past.

Tamsin Egerton (2009)

Tamsin Egerton is an English actress best known for her roles in the films St. Trinian’s and Chalet Girl.

Barnes was linked to her in 2009 after they were seen hanging out together in Australia. Although there were rumors of a romance between them, Egerton denied that they were dating and said that she had tried to set him up with one of her friends.

Amanda Seyfried (2013)

Amanda Seyfried is an American actress and singer who starred in the film Mamma Mia! and the TV series Big Love.

The actor dated her in 2013 after they worked together on the film The Big Wedding. However, their relationship did not last long, and they broke up later that year.

Felicity Jones (2014)

Felicity Jones is a British actress best known for her roles in the films The Theory of Everything and Rogue One: A Star Wars Story.

Barnes was linked to her in 2014 after they were seen together at the Britannia Awards and in Los Angeles. Although there were rumors of a romance between them, they were not seen together again after that.

Meganne Young (2017)

Meganne Young is a South African actress who has appeared in the TV series Black Mirror and the film The Kissing Booth.

Barnes was linked to her in 2017 after they were seen together at the Wimbledon tennis tournament and the Serpentine Summer Party in London. However, it’s unclear whether they were actually dating or just good friends, as neither of them confirmed their relationship publicly.

Julianne Hough (2020)

Julianne Hough is an American dancer and actress who is best known for her appearances on the TV series Dancing with the Stars.

As stated earlier, Barnes was linked to her in 2020 after they were seen together grabbing ice cream in Venice, CA, just two months after she filed for divorce from her ex-husband. Although they never confirmed their relationship, it’s believed that they had been friends for some time.

Ben Barnes’ bio: How old is Ben Barnes now? His age, height, and net worth

Ben Barnes was born on August 20, 1981, in London, England. As of February 2023, he is currently 41 years old. His real name or birth name is Benjamin Thomas Barnes.

The talented actor stands tall at the height of 6 feet (1.83m).

Moreover, Barnes is the son of Tricia Barnes, a relationship therapist, and Thomas Barnes, a professor of psychiatry. He also has a younger brother named Jack.

For his education, he attended two independent schools for boys: Homefield Preparatory School in Sutton and King’s College School in Wimbledon. He later studied drama and English literature at Kingston University in Kingston-upon-Thames and graduated with BA Honours in 2004.

As for his wealth, his work in the movies and TV shows has helped him earn handsomely and amass a decent net worth. According to Celebrity Net Worth, he has a net worth of $2 million presently.
